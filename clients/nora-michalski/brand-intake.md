## 1. The Offer

**What they sell:** The Greenlight Method™ — a proprietary, private psychological protocol delivered in 1–2 sessions. It removes the emotional charge attached to avoided business actions (procrastination, self-sabotage). Explicitly positioned as "not coaching, and not therapy."

**Core outcome the customer is buying:** The ability to take aligned, unblocked action on high-leverage or money-generating activities they've been avoiding — without willpower, hacks, or internal struggle. Secondary benefits include restored self-trust, more energy, better sleep, and improved client attraction.

**Price point & commitment:**
- $2,000 one-time (premium, single-payment)
- Includes: 1–2 private sessions + bonus audio suite for nervous system reinforcement + 30-day email accountability/implementation support + day-30 follow-up result-stabilization audit
- Guarantees: 7-day money-back + protocol guarantee (extra sessions at no charge if emotional charge doesn't drop significantly within 1–2 sessions)

**Vertical:** ☒ B2B/professional services (psychology-based performance protocol)

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:** Established entrepreneurs, executives, coaches, consultants, and senior professionals — high-achievers who are educated and capable but chronically procrastinating or avoiding one or more specific high-leverage, money-generating business actions.

**Secondary segments worth separating:**
1. Content creators and thought leaders (visibility avoidance)
2. Corporate executives / senior professionals (decision paralysis, career-defining actions)
3. Coaches and consultants (ironic meta-problem: they help others but can't help themselves)

**Problem state when they start looking:** They know exactly what they should be doing. They have the skills. They're frustrated with themselves. The pattern of avoidance has eroded their self-trust. They may have already tried productivity systems, therapy, or traditional coaching — without lasting results on this specific issue.

**Where leads come from today:** Not confirmed; likely organic search, referrals, and Nora's personal network/content. The free assessment is a self-serve inbound entry point.

**How they describe success in their own words:** "I just did the thing." Relief, not struggle. Energy back. Feeling like themselves again. Clients attract more naturally.

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:** Days to a few weeks.

**Funnel path:**
1. Discovery (organic / referral / assessment)
2. Free 15-question self-assessment ("Find Out What's Stopping You") — hosted on external tool
3. Apply for a demo/alignment call
4. Alignment call with Nora (qualification + close)
5. Enrollment at $2,000

**Free offer / trial:** Yes — free online assessment + free demo/alignment call (application-gated, not open-calendar).

**Who/what closes the deal:** Nora closes personally on the alignment call. This is a high-touch, 1:1 sales motion — not self-serve.

**#1 objection before buying:**
- **Price:** $2,000 is premium for a 1–2 session protocol. Site handles this directly: "not the cheapest option. It is the most effective."
- **Category skepticism:** "Is this therapy? Coaching? What exactly am I buying?" Buyers need education on the mechanism before they trust the outcome claim.

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:** A measurable, felt drop in emotional charge on the specific avoided action — experienced in the body during or immediately after the first private session. It's visceral, not intellectual.

**How long / self-serve or assisted:** Assisted and fast — within the first 1–2 sessions. The speed of result is a core differentiation claim.

**Implication for Campaign 1 (Welcome & Onboarding):** Emails before and between sessions should prime clients to notice and trust the somatic shift; post-session emails should reinforce the result and anchor the new identity.

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** No — one-time protocol. Revenue model is transactional.

**Renewal / repeat cycle:** No formal renewal. However: protocol guarantee creates a natural extension touchpoint if results need reinforcement.

**What "expansion" looks like:** Not publicly visible. Likely: referrals from satisfied clients, additional work for new blocks that surface, or future programs/group offerings (speculative). Alumni are a high-value referral source.

**Churn / drop-off problem:** Post-program drift if clients don't act while the emotional shift is fresh. The 30-day accountability window and day-30 audit are the guardrails, but there's no ongoing community or subscription to hold them.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal:** Likely full lifecycle — more qualified leads into the top of funnel + better conversion from demo call applicants.

**Target metrics:** Demo call booking rate from assessment completers; alignment call → enrollment conversion rate. Baselines unknown.

**Brand voice / tone:**
- Warm, direct, authority-based without arrogance
- Conversational; Nora uses personal story and vulnerability
- Avoids therapy-speak or coaching clichés
- Results language grounded in high-achiever identity ("you already know what to do")
- Spiritual undertone (Buddhist practitioner) but not foregrounded
- Charitable ethos (1% to anti-trafficking + homeless youth) — subtle but authentic

**Compliance constraints:** No regulated vertical. Standard CAN-SPAM / GDPR. Testimonials carry a "results not typical" disclaimer — maintain that standard in email social proof. No HIPAA.

**Sending volume:** Unknown; likely a small, high-quality list appropriate for a premium 1:1 offer.

## Output: First 3 Campaigns to Build

Given all gaps and the default priority order:

1. **Lead-Magnet Nurture** — Assessment completers are the highest-intent cold leads. A 5–7 email sequence from assessment result → demo call application is the fastest revenue lever.
2. **Trial/Consult Conversion** — Demo call applicants who don't show or don't enroll need a short follow-up sequence (2–3 emails) addressing the price and category-skepticism objections.
3. **Welcome & Onboarding** — Post-enrollment sequence priming new clients for their first session and anchoring the result when it lands; sets up referral ask at day 30.
