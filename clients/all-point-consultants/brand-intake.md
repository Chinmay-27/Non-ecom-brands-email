# Brand Intake — All Point Consultants
**URL:** https://www.allpointconsultants.com  
**Founder:** Glenn Grahl  
**Location:** North Fort Myers, FL  
**Date researched:** 2026-06-04

---

## 1. The Offer

**What they sell:** One-on-one financial wellness consulting and business formation guidance, delivered through named service tracks:
- **Clarity Track** — one-time assessment + written recommendations
- **Guided Track** — ongoing consulting with structured follow-up support
- **Business Readiness Track** — entity structuring, credit foundation, funding readiness milestones

Through the sub-brand (go.allpointconsultants.com) they also offer business credit building, financing matchmaking (SBA, equipment, revenue-based, credit line hybrid up to $150K), and startup financing.

Supported by: secure client portal, learning platform, professional network access.

**Core outcome the customer is buying:** Move from financial confusion and reactive decisions to a clear, written plan — understanding how personal credit, business credit, and financial systems connect, and having a structured path forward.

**Price point & commitment:** No public pricing. Likely mid-to-high ticket retainer or project fee. Multi-phase engagement (Discovery → Plan → Implementation → Review). Guided Track = recurring relationship; Clarity Track = one-off.

**Vertical:** ☑ B2B/Professional (primary) + individuals with financial/credit challenges

---

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:** Individuals and small business owners (US) who feel overwhelmed by overlapping personal/business finances, damaged or thin credit, or unclear funding paths. Often triggered by a specific financial event — loan denial, business launch, maxed personal credit — rather than planning proactively.

**Secondary segments:**
1. **Individuals** — personal credit rebuild, budgeting clarity, general financial wellness
2. **Business owners** — entity structuring, business credit establishment, funding readiness
3. **Personal-business crossover** — entrepreneurs with personal credit entangled in business liabilities

**Pain when they start looking:**
- Received financial directives without understanding the "why"
- Finances feel reactive and out of control
- Unsure whether personal vs. business credit is properly separated
- Been burned by quick-fix credit repair schemes before
- Decision paralysis from contradictory advice online

**Lead sources:** Likely SEO, referrals, and organic content. Lead magnets (free PDFs) suggest ad-driven top-of-funnel or social outreach.

**How clients describe success:** "I finally understand what I need to do," "I have a plan and I'm not guessing anymore," "My business is set up the right way."

---

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:** Weeks — multiple touchpoints (PDF download or assessment → follow-up → consultation → proposal → onboarding)

**Free trial/consult:** Yes — **free 15-minute assessment** booked via TidyCal. Also two free PDF lead magnets as top-of-funnel entry.

**Who/what closes the deal:** Discovery call with consultant → track recommendation → client portal onboarding. Explicitly low-pressure: "no automatic enrollments, no obligations."

**#1 objection / hesitation:** Skepticism ("Is this just another credit repair scheme?"), cost uncertainty (no public pricing), and "I can figure this out myself."

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:** The first structured consultation where the client sees their financial picture mapped clearly — credit profile, business structure, funding readiness — and leaves with a written plan and specific prioritized next steps.

**How long / self-serve or assisted:** Assisted. Happens during or immediately after the free 15-minute assessment, or in the first full paid session. Not self-serve.

**Campaign implication:** Welcome & Onboarding must drive toward booking and completing that first session. Pre-session prep emails ("here's what to bring / expect") will increase show rates and first-session quality.

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** Yes — Guided Track implies ongoing retainer/session cadence. Client portal access is ongoing.

**Renewal/repeat cycle:** Unknown cadence; likely monthly or quarterly check-ins within the Guided Track. Business financing programs may trigger repeat engagement as the client scales.

**Expansion looks like:** Clarity Track → Guided Track upgrade; personal financial wellness → business formation/credit track; professional network referrals generating new service needs.

**Churn/drop-off problem:** Long multi-phase cycles with unclear milestones lead to disengagement between sessions. Clients who don't take action between touchpoints lose momentum and go cold.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Assumed primary goal:** More conversions/bookings (free assessment → paid track) + lead nurture (PDF downloads → assessment)

**Target metrics:** Assessment booking rate from lead magnet downloads; show-up rate for booked assessments; free → paid conversion rate

**Brand voice / tone:** Calm, authoritative, educational. Never fear-based or salesy. "Trusted advisor in your corner." Clear plain language on complex financial topics. Founder-led warmth (Glenn Grahl's voice). No jargon; prefers analogies. Reassuring over urgent.

**Compliance constraints:** Financial consulting (not registered investment advice) — include standard financial disclaimer. CAN-SPAM required. No HIPAA. Avoid specific yield/return promises or language that reads as regulated financial advice. Florida LLC — check state disclosure requirements.

**Sending volume:** Unknown — start conservatively given unknown list health.

## Output: First 3 Campaigns to Build

1. **Lead-Magnet Nurture** — Both PDFs are live and likely generating downloads with zero follow-up. A 5–7 email sequence from download → education → assessment booking is the fastest revenue unlock.
2. **Welcome & Onboarding** — Anyone who books or creates a portal account needs a structured sequence anchoring the aha moment (first consultation) and setting 4-phase expectations.
3. **Trial/Consult Conversion** — Pre-assessment prep emails (increase show rate) + post-no-show recovery + post-assessment follow-up to close into Clarity or Guided Track.
