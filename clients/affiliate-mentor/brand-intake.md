# Brand Intake — Affiliate Mentor
**URL:** https://www.affiliatementor.com | **Location:** Tallahassee, FL | **Date:** 2026-06-04

---

## 1. The Offer

**What does the brand actually sell?**
A tiered membership platform at the intersection of email list-building software and affiliate marketing education. Three products:
- **Spark** — $25 one-time entry; a 2×2 matrix product that cycles to earn $50 (plus matching bonuses). Low-barrier proof-of-concept.
- **CORE Membership** — $49.50/month or $397/year. The flagship product: done-for-you marketing system, pre-built lead capture pages, GetResponse email integration, ClickCash™ email monetization ($0.10/click), monthly Platinum Workshops, training vault, and a Facebook community.
- **VIP Marketer's Club** — $297/year (added on top of CORE). Doubles ClickCash™ to $0.20/click, unlocks up to $2,500 in complimentary traffic, and adds certification programs (Traffic Generation Mastery, Email Marketing Mastery, Funnels).

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**
Build a large, monetizable email list and earn predictable affiliate commissions — without building funnels, writing emails, or figuring out traffic from scratch.

**Price point & commitment:**
- One-off entry: $25 (Spark), $297 (VIP add-on)
- Recurring: $49.50/month or $397/year (CORE)
- Mid-to-low ticket; recurring revenue anchored at CORE level

**Vertical:** ☑ Education/membership (online marketing training + commissionable platform)

---

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:**
Aspiring online entrepreneurs and beginner-to-intermediate affiliate marketers — typically side-hustlers or career-changers who want a structured, done-for-you system to generate remote income. Often previously burned by courses or programs that didn't deliver results.

**Secondary segments worth separating:**
1. **Beginners / explorers** — drawn by the $25 Spark, testing online income with minimal commitment
2. **Active affiliates** — already doing some affiliate marketing but want a better system and consistent traffic (CORE buyers)
3. **Serious builders / recruiters** — focused on scaling commissions through team building and traffic (VIP + traffic package buyers)

**What problem are they in when they start looking?**
Inconsistent or near-zero affiliate income despite real time investment — frustrated by traffic that doesn't convert, overwhelmed by the technical complexity of email list setup, and lacking a clear framework or community to learn from.

**Where do leads come from today?**
Primarily referral-driven (member referral links / username-based signup required). Social media (Facebook community, TikTok, YouTube affiliates posting reviews). Review SEO traffic.

**How do they describe success in their own words?**
"Making money while I sleep." "Getting my first $500 week." "Building a real list I own." "Finally having a system that does the work for me."

---

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:** Same day to a few days (low-price self-serve entry, no sales call required)

**Free trial / intro offer:**
No free trial. Two soft-entry options:
1. **Free reseller participation** — join the compensation plan without buying any product (limited earning ability)
2. **$25 Spark** — functions as the effective paid trial; low enough to reduce friction significantly

**Who/what closes the deal?**
Self-serve signup via referral link. The referring affiliate's testimonials, YouTube reviews, and social proof do the heavy lifting. No salesperson or call required.

**#1 objection or hesitation before buying:**
*"Is this a legitimate business or just another MLM/pyramid scheme?"* — skepticism about income claims, concerns about hidden costs, wariness from prior bad experiences with online income programs.

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**The activation event:**
Getting their first opt-in lead through their configured landing page — OR seeing their first ClickCash™ payout from an email click. Both prove the system "actually works" and build the trust needed to stay.

**How long does it take?**
Self-serve, but requires setup: connect GetResponse ($19/mo external), configure the landing page, activate email follow-up sequences. Without guidance, members can stall here and never activate. Assisted onboarding is critical.

*→ This is the anchor for Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?**
Yes — CORE membership ($49.50/month or $397/year) is the core recurring revenue engine.

**Renewal/repeat cycle:**
Monthly or annual. Annual converts at $397 (saves ~$200 vs monthly).

**What does "expansion" look like?**
1. Spark → CORE upgrade (the most important upsell)
2. CORE monthly → CORE annual (reduces churn, increases LTV)
3. CORE → add VIP ($297/yr) for doubled ClickCash and free traffic
4. Traffic package purchases from the Elite Affiliate Traffic Store (wholesale, up to 50% off)

**Churn / drop-off problem:**
Members who don't recruit fast enough — and therefore see no commission income — lose motivation to pay the monthly fee. The core churn story: *joined for the income, stalled on setup, saw no results in 60–90 days, cancelled.* Annual subscribers retain far better; the monthly tier is the leak.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal this quarter:** Full lifecycle — convert more Spark buyers to CORE, reduce CORE monthly churn, reactivate lapsed members.

**Target metric & baseline:** Unknown — CTR, CORE conversion rate from Spark, monthly churn rate on CORE subscription.

**Brand voice / tone:**
Motivational and results-focused. Uses specific dollar figures ($20, $200, $500/cycle) to anchor aspiration. Friendly and accessible — not corporate. Emphasizes simplicity ("done-for-you"). Needs consistent income-disclosure language to stay FTC-compliant and counter MLM skepticism without undermining excitement.

**Compliance constraints:**
- **CAN-SPAM**: Physical address (Tallahassee, FL) required; unsubscribe mechanism mandatory
- **FTC income disclosure**: All emails referencing earnings, commissions, or income potential must include a disclosure (e.g., "Results are not typical. Income depends on individual effort and is not guaranteed.")
- No healthcare/HIPAA or financial-services constraints

**Sending volume:** Unknown — platform dependent; GetResponse integration suggests standard ESP limits apply.
