Your 2-minute job-day prep

A little prep goes a long way. Check off these four things before we arrive and the crew can get straight to work:

🚘

Move vehicles away from the work area

Park in the street or at the far end of the driveway so our hoses and equipment can reach everything.
🔑

Unlock any gates or fenced areas

Side gates, back yard access — if we need to get around the house, a locked gate means a delayed job.
🐾

Bring pets inside

High-pressure water is no fun for curious pups or cats. Keep them comfortable indoors while we work.
🚲

Clear the work area

Move lawn furniture, potted plants, toys, or decorations away from siding and walkways so nothing gets in the way (or gets wet!).

You don't need to be home — most exterior jobs don't require it. Just make sure access is open and you're reachable by phone if we have a quick question.
