## 1. The Offer

**What do they sell?**  
Professional exterior cleaning services for residential and commercial properties in the Acworth / Marietta, GA area. Core services:
- House exterior / siding washing
- Paver cleaning & restoration
- Roof washing
- Commercial power washing

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**  
A visibly clean, bright, cared-for property exterior — restored curb appeal — without the homeowner doing any of the work themselves. The emotional outcome is pride in how their home looks.

**Price point & commitment:**  
Quote-based; no pricing published on the site. Free no-obligation quotes via web form, phone (678-223-0550), or email (service@freshviewser.com). Jobs are one-off services; repeat revenue comes from seasonal re-bookings, not a subscription model.

**Vertical:** ☑ Local & service

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:**  
Homeowners in Acworth / Marietta, GA — typically 30–60 years old — who notice their siding, roof, or pavers have become visibly dirty and want a fast, reliable professional to handle it.

**Secondary segments:**  
Small commercial property owners and landlords needing exterior upkeep on rental or business properties.

**What problem are they in when they start looking?**  
- Visibly dingy or stained exterior (siding, driveway, pavers, roof streaks)
- Preparing to list a home for sale or rent
- HOA or municipality complaint about property appearance
- Hosting an event and wanting the home to look its best
- Too busy, too old, or physically limited to DIY

**Where do leads come from today?**  
Unknown from site data — likely Google Search / local SEO ("pressure washing near me"), Google Business Profile reviews, and word-of-mouth referrals. Site is optimized for inbound quote requests.

**How do customers describe success?**  
"My house looks brand new." "I didn't realize how dirty it had gotten." "Great results, would hire again."

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:** Same day to a few days  

**Free trial / consult / first-session offer:**  
Yes — free no-obligation quote. Customer submits a form or calls, gets pricing, then books.

**Who/what closes the deal?**  
Manual: owner or team follows up with the quote via phone or email → customer confirms and books a date.

**#1 objection or hesitation before buying:**  
Price vs. renting equipment and DIYing it; secondary hesitation is trusting a stranger to work around their property without causing damage.

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:**  
The completed job walk-around — the moment the customer sees the dramatic before/after transformation of their property exterior.

**How long does it take?**  
A few hours on job day; fully assisted (the crew does all the work). The value is visible immediately upon job completion.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Target metrics (baseline unknown):**

Given that everything is missing and the business model is quote → book → job → re-book:

1. **Welcome & Onboarding** — Triggered by quote request or first booking; set expectations, build trust before the job, celebrate the result after with a review ask
2. **Trial/Consult Conversion** — Automated follow-up sequence for quote requests that don't book within 48 hours
