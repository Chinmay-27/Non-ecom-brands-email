Credential Stacking Opportunity
The OPE-CS Clinical Specialist designation is one path away
The OPE-CS — Orthopedic Clinical Specialist — is NBCOS's combined credential, earned by passing all three specialty exams. If you've already passed the OPE-C, you've completed the first step. Two more exams separate you from a designation that signals mastery across orthopedic clinical, surgical, and radiographic competencies.
