## 1. The Offer

**What does the brand actually sell?**  
Leadership Books is a dual-sided business:

1. **Book Retail + Membership** — Curated online bookstore selling physical leadership, business, finance, Christian, and political thought-leader books. Also offers the *Better Book Club*, a quarterly subscription (6 curated books/quarter, member pricing, author events, LinkedIn group, free shipping).

2. **Author Publishing Services** — A hybrid publishing house positioning itself between traditional and self-publishing. Services include editorial, cover/interior design, distribution to major retailers, strategic marketing, media placement, Speakers Bureau representation, and a Book Launch Mastermind program. Authors keep 100% of their rights.

**What's the core outcome the customer is buying?**  
- *Readers:* Stay ahead as a leader through high-signal, peer-curated reading without wasting time on the wrong books.  
- *Authors:* Transform a personal message into a professionally published book that builds a speaking career, grows influence, and launches a movement — without surrendering rights or settling for a DIY result.

**Price point & commitment:**

**Vertical:** Education/membership (book club + retail) + B2B/professional services (publishing)

## 2. The Audience

**Primary customer / ICP:**  
Two distinct ICPs exist on the same platform:

1. **Avid Leader/Reader** — Business executives, entrepreneurs, managers, and high-achievers who consume 40–60 books/year and want curated, high-signal recommendations. Top-level executives read around 60 books annually (site's own stat).

2. **Aspiring Author/Thought Leader** — Business leaders, coaches, speakers, pastors, and professionals with a message to share who want professional publishing support without surrendering their rights or accepting a cookie-cutter self-publishing result.

**Secondary segments worth separating:**
- Book Club subscribers (recurring, high-engagement readers)
- One-off book buyers (acquisition pool for Club upsell)
- Published authors seeking re-launch, Speakers Bureau representation, or book launch support
- Pastors/ministry leaders (Christian leadership is a distinct category with 334+ titles)

**What problem are they in when they start looking?**  
- Readers: Overwhelmed by book volume; algorithm-fatigue from Amazon; want community around reading, not just a list.
- Authors: Rejected by traditional publishing or burned by poor self-publishing platforms — lose rights, earn low royalties, get zero marketing support, and their message disappears in noise.

**Where do leads come from today?**  
YouTube (@leadershipbooks), TikTok (@ldrshipbooks), Trustpilot reviews, organic search (SEO-focused collections), Author Summit event, and possibly word-of-mouth from satisfied authors.

**How do they describe success in their own words?**  
- Readers: "I'm reading what top leaders are reading." "I'm growing as a leader every quarter."
- Authors: "My book landed national media." "I launched my speaking career." "I changed lives with my message."

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:**  
- Books / Book Club: Same day (self-serve Shopify checkout)
- Publishing packages: Weeks to months (webcast → free consultation → proposal → purchase)

**Free trial / consult / demo?**  
Yes — two entry points for publishing services:
- Free *Publishing Services Webcast* (on-demand webinar)
- Free *Book Consultation* with the Acquisitions Team (booked call via discovery page)

For book buyers: $10 off first order (code: FIRST10) lowers purchase barrier.

**Who/what closes the deal?**  
- Books/Book Club: Self-serve checkout
- Publishing packages: The Acquisitions Team via discovery call → custom proposal

**#1 objection or hesitation before buying?**  
- Authors: "Why pay $15K when I can use Amazon KDP for almost nothing?" (cost vs. quality/support/rights trade-off)
- Book Club: "Why subscribe when I can pick books myself on Amazon?" (curation value proposition)

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**For Book Club members:**  
First curated quarterly shipment arrives — the quality and relevance of the selection validates the membership. Secondary aha: joining the members-only LinkedIn group and attending a live author event.

**For Publishing services leads:**  
The free webcast or discovery call — the moment when they realize Leadership Books is a genuine creative co-pilot (not just a print button), that they retain 100% of their rights, and that there's a real marketing strategy behind their book.

**For one-off book buyers:**  
Completing and acting on insights from their first purchase.

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?**  
Yes — primarily through the Better Book Club quarterly subscription. Publishing services are one-off high-ticket but create expansion opportunities.

**Renewal / repeat cycle length:** 90 days (quarterly subscription)

**What does "expansion" look like?**  
- Book Club member → Author Publishing Package (reader becomes author)
- One-off buyer → Book Club subscriber
- Publishing client → Book Launch Mastermind → Speakers Bureau → Author Summit
- Author Summit ticket → publishing package conversion

**Churn / drop-off problem:**  
- Book Club: Members who accumulate unread books cancel (classic book-subscription churn)
- Publishing clients: Complete one package and don't re-engage for second book or launch mastermind
- One-off book buyers: Never return after first purchase (no follow-up visible)

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal:** Full lifecycle build (both reader and author journeys are underserved)

**Priority based on gaps:**  
Given two ICPs, sequencing should address each funnel:

**Reader/Book Club track:**
1. Welcome & Onboarding (new Book Club subscriber → activate LinkedIn group + set reading habit)
2. Win-Back / Re-Engagement (lapsed buyers and churned club members)
3. Renewal & Expansion (quarterly renewal prompt + upsell from reader → author)

**Author publishing track:**
1. Lead-Magnet Nurture (webcast viewer or free consultation lead → publishing package)
2. Trial/Consult Conversion (post-discovery call follow-up sequence)
3. Booking & Reminders (no-show prevention for discovery calls)

**Brand voice / tone:** Aspirational and empowering. Warm co-pilot energy, not a cold vendor. Mission-driven. "Become More." "Let Your Story Shine." "Turn your book into a movement." Avoids corporate stiffness — speaks to ambition with genuine enthusiasm.

**Compliance constraints:** Standard CAN-SPAM / GDPR. No healthcare, finance, or legal vertical constraints. International buyers present (India INR shown as default currency in Playwright session — global audience).

**Sending volume:** Unknown — infer low-to-medium list given Shopify store scale; likely under 50K subscribers.
