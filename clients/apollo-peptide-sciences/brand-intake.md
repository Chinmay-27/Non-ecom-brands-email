## 1. The Offer

**What do they sell?**
Research-grade peptides and chemicals in lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder form, sold direct-to-consumer via e-commerce. Currently 23+ SKUs. Key products include:
- BPC-157, GHK-Cu, SNAP-8, FOX04-DRI, Epitalon, IGF-1 LR3 (performance/longevity)
- NAD+ 500mg (cellular energy/research)
- GLP-1 S / Semaglutide 5mg, 10mg, 15mg (metabolic research)
- GLP-2 T / Tirzepatide 15mg, 20mg, 60mg; multi-packs (10-pack, 5-pack)
- Retatrutide (emerging GLP class research)

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**
Pharmaceutical-grade purity (≥98% HPLC-verified, often ≥99%) with a transparent, independently-issued Certificate of Analysis — so researchers can trust the inputs and get credible results.

**Price point & commitment:**
- Range: ~$40–$300+ per vial; BPC-157 10mg at $59.99
- Free shipping on orders over $200
- One-off purchase model; no subscription
- Multi-unit packs available for volume buyers
- Payment: credit card and cryptocurrency accepted
- Orders dispatched within 48 hours; USPS Priority/Express

**Vertical:** ☑ Other — Research chemicals / peptide e-commerce (adjacent to B2B/professional and health/science)

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:**
Independent researchers, academic lab professionals, and scientifically-literate biohackers sourcing research-grade peptides for in-vitro or laboratory studies. Particular concentration around longevity, metabolic health, and performance peptides.

**Secondary segments:**
1. Academic/institutional researchers who need documented COAs for study records and compliance
2. Longevity and biohacking community (GHK-Cu, NAD+, Epitalon, FOX04-DRI buyers)
3. Repeat bulk buyers studying GLP-1/GLP-2 analogs (Semaglutide, Tirzepatide, Retatrutide)

**Problem they're in when they start looking:**
Searching for a trustworthy vendor in a low-trust space — most peptide suppliers have murky sourcing, no published COAs, or inconsistent purity. The researcher needs verified, affordable inputs and doesn't want to waste a study on impure compounds.

**Where leads come from today:**
- Organic search (product and brand name terms rank on Google)
- Review/comparison sites (Pepti7 #6 ranking, Outliyr top 13 list)
- Social media (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook presence active)
- Coupon/deal aggregators (SimplyCodes, CouponsVilla — indicates price-conscious traffic)
- Word-of-mouth in research/biohacking communities

**How they describe success:**
"Pure product, exactly what the spec says, arrived fast, COA included, price made sense."

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → purchase:** Same-day to a few days. Fully self-serve e-commerce.

**Free trial / consult / demo?**
No. Entry offer is the newsletter discount: **10% off storewide + free shipping unlocked** for email signup. No free samples or consultation model.

**Who/what closes the deal?**
Self-serve add-to-cart. The conversion drivers are:
1. Third-party COA transparency (≥98% purity documented)
2. Competitive price point vs. established vendors
3. Newsletter discount code (10% off) reducing first-order friction
4. Third-party vendor reviews (Pepti7 8.4/10 rating)

**#1 objection before buying:**
*"Is the purity actually what they claim?"* — Addressed by publishing HPLC-verified COAs from independent labs before sale, with detailed technical data sheets.

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:**
Receiving the order and reviewing the Certificate of Analysis — purity confirmed at ≥98% HPLC, product matches the spec. The researcher can now proceed with confidence.

**How long / self-serve or assisted?**
1–5 days (shipping time). Fully unassisted — COA is packaged with product. Storage instructions provided (lyophilized: cool/dry; reconstituted: refrigerate 36–46°F, use within 4–6 weeks).

*This becomes the anchor of the Welcome & Onboarding campaign: confirm the order, pre-sell the COA moment, deliver reconstitution/storage guidance so the first use succeeds.*

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal (assumed):**
Full lifecycle build — convert newsletter subscribers to first buyers, retain buyers for repeat orders, and win back lapsed customers.

**Target metrics:**
- Newsletter-to-first-purchase conversion rate
- Repeat purchase rate (reorder within 60–90 days)
- Average order value (push toward $200 free shipping threshold)

**Brand voice / tone:**
Scientific and authoritative with approachable clarity. Premium but not exclusionary. Confident in quality claims without hyperbole. Uses research-community lexicon. Strictly avoids therapeutic, medical, or dosing language. Think: "trusted lab supplier" not "wellness brand."

**Compliance constraints:**
- All email copy must include: *"For research purposes only — not for human consumption."*
- Zero health claims, therapeutic claims, or dosing guidance in any email
- Not a pharmacy or compounding facility (503A/503B) — do not imply medical use
- CAN-SPAM compliant: clear opt-out in every send
- FDA regulatory environment for research peptides is active and evolving — stay strictly within research framing at all times

**Sending volume:**
Unknown — defer to ESP limits and current list size.
