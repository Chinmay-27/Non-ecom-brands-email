I know how it goes. The liturgical calendar keeps turning, the rehearsal schedule fills up, and somewhere between Ordinary Time and the next big feast, an email from a small music press slips quietly to the bottom of the inbox. No guilt — that is simply what volunteer parish ministry looks like.
