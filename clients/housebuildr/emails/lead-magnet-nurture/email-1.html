Why most homeowners stall — and how the guide cuts through it

Going solar in California isn't complicated because the technology is hard. It's complicated because there are five moving pieces that all have to work together at once:

▶

Incentives that expire or change — the 30% federal Investment Tax Credit exists under current IRA schedules, but the rules matter. The guide covers exactly what's available in 2026.
▶

Permits and city approvals — every LA-area municipality has its own process; without a licensed contractor managing this, timelines slip by months.
▶

Financing options — cash purchase, PACE, solar loan, and lease/PPA each have a different ROI profile. The guide compares them side by side.
▶

Equipment tiers — not all panels perform equally in Southern California's climate; the guide explains what to look for and what to avoid.
▶

Choosing an installer you can trust — a licensed, experienced contractor makes the difference between a system that performs for 25+ years and one that creates headaches.
