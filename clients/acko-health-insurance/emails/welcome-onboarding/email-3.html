What actually happens when you call us

The thing that breaks people during a medical emergency is uncertainty — "who do I call? what do I say? will it be approved?" Here's the exact flow:

1

You (or someone with you) calls the 24/7 helpline

The number is on your digital policy card. One call connects you to the pre-authorisation desk — a dedicated team trained for exactly this moment, available day or night.
2

We contact the hospital directly

Our team raises the pre-authorisation request with the hospital's insurance desk. You don't need to coordinate between us — we do that. For planned procedures, pre-auth can be set up hours before you even check in.
✓

You check out with a ₹0 bill

Once pre-auth is approved, the hospital bills Acko directly. You sign the discharge summary — that's it. No reimbursement forms, no running around for receipts, no waiting for a cheque.
