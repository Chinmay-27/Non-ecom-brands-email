How cashless treatment works
1

Walk up to the insurance desk

Every network hospital has one. Show them your digital Acko policy (it's right in the app, under your name). That's it. You don't need a physical card, a printout, or an agent.
2

We pre-authorise, usually within hours

The hospital sends us the treatment details. Our pre-authorisation team reviews it — 24/7, 365 days. We approve the cover directly with the hospital. You don't need to be on the phone or fill out a single form.
3

You walk out. We settle the bill.

At discharge, we pay the hospital directly. Zero co-pay. Zero reimbursement claim to file later. You focus on recovering — not on paperwork or payments.

100% of the bill. ₹0 from your pocket at the desk.
