⚠️ If your policy lapses, here's what you lose

• Waiting-period credit resets. Years you've already served — the 2-year waiting period on pre-existing conditions, the 30-day initial waiting period — gone. You start from zero with any new insurer.
• Continuity benefits disappear. Any ongoing condition may be treated as a new pre-existing condition by another insurer.
• A gap in coverage — even for one day — counts as a lapse under IRDAI portability rules. You are unprotected the moment your policy expires.
