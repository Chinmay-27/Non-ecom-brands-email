A preview of what's inside

These are the five questions the guide walks you through — in plain language, with real context for each one.

Question 1

Do you need liquidity now — or just prefer it?

Urgency changes the math. The guide helps you size up your actual cash need against the cost of selling today versus waiting.

Question 2

How solid is your buyer?

Payment history, credit profile, and equity position all tell you how much default risk you're carrying today — whether you feel it or not.

Question 3

What is managing this note actually costing you?

Time, attention, and anxiety have a real value. Most note holders underestimate the ongoing cost of staying in the deal.

Question 4

What would the cash unlock for you?

A lump sum is most valuable when it moves something — a retirement plan, a debt payoff, an investment, an estate. If there's no compelling use for it, that's worth knowing too.

Question 5

Full sale or partial?

Not everything has to be all-or-nothing. Selling a defined number of future payments lets you get cash today while keeping the note — and the future income stream — if you want it back later.
