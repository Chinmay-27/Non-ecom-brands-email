Move-Day Prep Checklist

Four things that make the difference between a smooth job and a stressful one:

1

Park the truck as close to the entrance as possible

Every extra foot of distance adds time and wear. Driveway, loading dock, or right at the curb — whatever gets the tailgate closest to the door.
2

Clear the path from door to truck

Boxes in the hallway, kids' toys on the stairs, cars in the way — clear it all. A clean walkway means a faster, safer job.
3

Label fragile items clearly — especially the piano

Tape a "FRAGILE" note on anything that needs extra care. If you have a piano, let us know the model ahead of time — we've been moving pianos since 2011 and we want to know exactly what we're handling.
4

Be present (or have someone there) when we arrive

We'll knock, confirm we're at the right spot, and get straight to work. You don't need to direct us — just be available for a quick hello and any questions that come up.
