## 1. The Offer

**What does the brand sell?**  
Residential real estate representation — buyer's agent services and home listing (seller) services in the Atlanta metro market. Secondary: merchandise and moving supplies, real estate career recruitment.

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**  
A calm, stress-free home purchase or sale with a trusted local agent who explains everything plainly and stays in their corner long after the keys change hands.

**Price point & commitment:**  
Commission-based; no upfront fee. Typical Atlanta market rates (~2.5–3% buyer-side; ~5–6% total on listings). Average transaction value likely $300k–$600k+. Engagement is per-transaction but cultivated for lifetime relationship and referrals.

**Vertical:** ☑ Local & service (residential real estate)

---

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:**  
Atlanta-area first-time homebuyers and current homeowners looking to sell or upsize — people who value clear communication, relationship, and hand-holding over a transactional, purely numbers-driven experience.

**Secondary segments worth separating:**
- Move-up or downsizing sellers (existing homeowners)
- Out-of-state relocators moving to Atlanta
- Past clients and warm referral sources (sphere of influence)

**Problem they're in when they start looking:**  
Feeling overwhelmed by Atlanta's fast-moving market, unsure of the process, and worried about making a costly mistake without someone they can actually trust.

**Where do leads come from today?**  
Social media (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube), referrals from past clients, Keller Williams network, and organic site traffic.

**How they describe success:**  
"It was stress-free." / "He made everything easy and explained every question." / "Got it under contract quickly with multiple offers."

---

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:**  
☑ Weeks–months (home search: 30–90 days; contract-to-close: 30–60 days)

**Free trial / consult / demo:**  
Yes — free buyer or listing consultation bookable via Calendly (no commitment, no cost). Also free home buyer questionnaire and home seller questionnaire on-site.

**Who/what closes the deal?**  
A booked call/consultation with Aaron leads to signing a Buyer Representation Agreement or Listing Agreement.

**#1 objection before buying:**  
"I'm not ready yet" / "I want to shop around" / "I'm not sure about the market right now"

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:**  
The first consultation where the client feels heard and the intimidating process is made simple — or the first home tour where their anxiety visibly drops. For sellers: seeing the listing go live and professional marketing in action.

**How long / self-serve or assisted?**  
Assisted. Consultation is 30–60 minutes via Calendly; Aaron handles all steps personally.

*This becomes the anchor of Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding — nurture toward booking that first consult.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?**  
Not inherently. Each transaction is project-based. Revenue model depends on repeat transactions and referrals.

**Repeat cycle length:**  
Average homeowner moves every 5–7 years. Past clients are the highest-value long-term asset.

**What does "expansion" look like?**  
Referrals to friends and family; second transaction (upsizing, investment property, downsizing); agent-to-agent referral network.

**Churn / drop-off problem:**  
The 3–7 year gap between transactions is the core churn risk — clients forget about Aaron and accept the next referral or Instagram ad they encounter. No ongoing nurture means out of sight, out of mind.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal:**  
☑ More conversions/bookings (consult bookings) + better retention (past client re-engagement)

**Target metric & baseline:**  
Consult booking rate from lead; referral volume from past clients. Baseline unknown — to establish on kickoff.

**Brand voice / tone:**  
Warm, casual, southern-hospitable. Uses food and home metaphors ("Getting Cooking," "The Ingredients," "Let's Get to Moving"). Conversational, personal, avoids corporate real estate language. Copy should feel like a trusted friend who happens to know real estate cold.

**Compliance constraints:**  
CAN-SPAM / email opt-in required. Georgia real estate license disclosures in marketing materials. No HIPAA or GDPR-specific constraints.

**Sending volume:**  
Unknown — to confirm list size and platform limits on kickoff.
