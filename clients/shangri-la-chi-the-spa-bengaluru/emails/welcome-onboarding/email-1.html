You have found your way here, and we are glad.

CHI is the ancient Chinese concept of the universal life force — the energy that flows through everything, that is restored when the body is given stillness, warmth, and the wisdom of centuries of Asian healing tradition. That is the philosophy behind every treatment on our menu, every room on our third floor, every unhurried hour we hold for the guests who choose to arrive.

 

Where ancient wisdom meets the present moment

Our therapists are trained in the living traditions of Asian healing — the long, grounding strokes of Swedish massage, the precise energy-point work of Shiatsu, the balancing whole-body flow of Reflexology. Woven together into the CHI Signature treatments. Complemented by the Ayurvedic rituals that have endured for three thousand years: the warm oil pours of Abhyanga, the meditative stream of Shirodhara, the herb-compressed warmth of Pinda Sweda.

None of this is hurried. None of it is generic. And it begins long before the treatment itself — in the consultation, the aromatic oils, the quiet of the third floor, the simple act of having nowhere else to be.

 

A note about these notes

Short, occasional, never noise

You will not hear from us every day. You will not be sold to. What arrives in your inbox from CHI is sent with the same intention as the spa itself — to offer something worth pausing for. Seasonal treatments. A quiet thought on rest. An occasion to remember. And sometimes, simply, a reminder that stillness is still there when you need it.

If anything ever calls for a real conversation — a treatment recommendation, a question, a moment where you would rather speak to a person than click a link — simply reply to this email. Someone will answer.
