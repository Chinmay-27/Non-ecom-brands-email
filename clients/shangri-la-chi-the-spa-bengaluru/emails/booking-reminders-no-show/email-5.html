After your treatment

A few quiet requests from your therapist

The aromatic oils continue to work through the skin for several hours after you leave. To honour that:

 

Drink generously. Warm water or herbal tea — not cold — helps the body continue to release what was worked through.

 

Rest if you can. An hour of quiet — no screens, no demands — lets the nervous system settle into the work that was done.

 

Avoid the heat. A hot shower or intense exercise too soon can undo some of what the treatment gave you — tomorrow is soon enough.
