Finding your way

Staying with us at Shangri-La?

Take any lift to the 3rd floor. Our team will be at the CHI reception to welcome you. No check-in needed — simply arrive and let the day begin to slow.

Enter via the main hotel entrance on Palace Road. Our lobby team will direct you to the lifts — CHI is on the 3rd floor. Complimentary valet parking is available. Please ensure you arrive before 10:00 pm, as hotel access for spa guests closes at that hour.
