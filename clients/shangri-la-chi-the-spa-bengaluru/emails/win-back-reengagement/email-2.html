Since your last visit, we have added new seasonal rituals, deepened our Ayurvedic offerings, and brought in a collaboration that feels — if we may say so — entirely worth your time. Not a renovation. A quiet evolution, done the way CHI does most things: without announcement, without noise.

New this season

The Pinda Sweda Revival Ritual

Drawn from Kerala's classical Ayurvedic tradition, this 90-minute ritual uses boluses of rice and milk — warmed, kneaded, pressed in steady rhythms across the body — to dissolve accumulated tension in the deeper muscle layers. It is the treatment most often requested a second time before the first is even finished.

Particularly suited to those who carry the day in their shoulders and the week in their sleep.

Also new

Udwartana — The Grain Ritual

A dry herbal powder exfoliation rooted in Ayurveda — invigorating rather than indulgent, designed to reawaken circulation and leave the skin with a stillness you can feel rather than see. 60 minutes.

A collaboration

CHI × Shankara:
The Sandalwood Journey

We have partnered with Shankara — India's most respected Ayurvedic skincare house — to offer a 90-minute ritual that moves from a warm sandalwood oil application through a full-body massage and into a targeted face treatment using Shankara's cold-pressed botanical formulations.

The result is a quality of restoration that neither discipline achieves alone. The scent, if you have never encountered Mysore sandalwood worked into warm skin, is something to look forward to.

90 minutes  ·  ₹ 9,200 + taxes
