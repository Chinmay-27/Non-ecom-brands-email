Ninety Minutes

A fuller kind of stillness

The difference between a 60- and 90-minute CHI Signature session is not simply thirty minutes. It is the unhurried middle — that quiet stretch where the body finally releases what it has been holding. A 90-minute session allows your therapist to work with the full rhythm of the treatment, rather than arriving at depth just as the session draws to a close.

Add-On Rituals

A layer that changes everything

Some guests choose to arrive a step earlier — a full-body scrub before their massage, so the skin is prepared and the muscles more receptive. Others add a Shirodhara to their session: a steady warm stream of oil poured along the forehead, an ancient Ayurvedic practice that quiets a restless mind in a way that is difficult to describe until you have experienced it.

If any of this sounds right, the team can advise on the best pairing for your last treatment — just mention it when you book, or reply to this note.
