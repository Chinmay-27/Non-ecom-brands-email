Dear {{first_name}},

There is a particular kind of generosity that cannot be wrapped. It does not sit under a tree or arrive in a courier box. It is the gift of an afternoon that belongs entirely to someone else — their breath, their quiet, their unhurried return to themselves.

The festive season has a way of filling every hour. We find ourselves planning and gathering and celebrating until the very act of celebration becomes its own kind of exhaustion. This year, we would like to offer you something different — the gift of stillness, given to someone you love, or quietly, to yourself.

“They didn’t want to leave. Neither did we, when we saw their face afterwards.”

A guest, gifting to her mother

What follows is a quiet guide to gifting a CHI experience — the options, the rituals, and the one or two things worth knowing before you choose.
 

On gifting

A voucher, so they may choose their own quiet

Gift vouchers for CHI are available in any denomination — presented beautifully as a digital e-voucher and redeemable against any treatment on the spa menu. They carry no expiry pressure and no obligation to choose anything today. The recipient books when the moment is theirs.

It is, we think, a rather graceful way to say: I thought of you. Take an afternoon.

Featured this season

The Festive Spa Getaway

A curated seasonal package that pairs a full-body ritual with unhurried access to CHI’s hydrothermal sanctuary — the steam room, sauna and Jacuzzi — followed by a treatment chosen from our most restorative menu. A complete afternoon of belonging to no one’s calendar but your own.

For two

Some gifts are best when shared

CHI’s dedicated couples’ rooms allow two people to experience their treatments side by side — in the same unhurried quiet, without the city between them. Whether it is a partner, a parent, a closest friend, or a celebration long overdue, a shared afternoon here has a way of becoming the part of the season you remember most.

Our 7-treatment bundle — five treatments at the price of five, two gifted — is also a considered choice for someone for whom CHI is already a ritual, and deserves to continue being one.

A note on choosing: if you are unsure which treatment to suggest, a gift voucher is always the right answer. They will know, when they arrive and take a breath, exactly what they need. Our team is also happy to advise — simply reply to this note, or call the spa directly.
