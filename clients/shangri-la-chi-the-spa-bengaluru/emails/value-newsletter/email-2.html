In Conversation

Priya Nair

Senior Therapist  ·  CHI, The Spa

"I trained for three years before I touched a guest. First in Kerala — the traditional Ayurvedic lineage, the marma points, the oils. Then in Bangkok for Shiatsu. Then at Shangri-La's own CHI training programme, which is where the two traditions met for me and became something I could honestly call my own."

Priya has been with CHI Bengaluru for six years. She specialises in the CHI Signature Massage — that proprietary layering of Swedish flow, Shiatsu pressure-point work and Reflexology that the spa's founders built to bridge East and West without diluting either.

"The most common thing guests say after a session is: 'I didn't realise how much I was carrying.' That's the work. Not just the shoulders or the neck — the accumulated weight of being switched on all day. My job is to create a long enough pause that the body remembers it knows how to let go."

"An unhurried touch is the rarest thing in a city that is always moving."

Behind the Ritual

Before a session begins, Priya reads the health consultation form — not to categorise, but to listen. What someone writes about their week, their sleep, a recent flight, tells her more than any clinical intake. "I adjust the pressure and the sequence every single time. No two guests are the same, even if they book the same treatment."

The oils are blended according to the CHI system — warm Ylang Ylang and sandalwood for deep release, cooler peppermint and eucalyptus for invigoration. The room is heated. The ambient light holds at a specific low amber. None of this is accidental.

"By the time I begin, the environment has already done a third of the work," she says. "I just take it the rest of the way."

The CHI Standard

Every CHI therapist worldwide trains through the same programme — one rooted in ancient Asian healing traditions, refined across forty years of Shangri-La hospitality. What Priya brings to the table in Bengaluru is the same body of knowledge carried by a CHI therapist in Hong Kong, Kuala Lumpur, or Dubai.

This is not a hotel spa as a hotel add-on. It is a full wellness practice that happens to live inside one of the world's finest addresses.

If you have been meaning to find your way to the 3rd floor — or if it has been a little while since you last did — consider this a quiet reminder that Priya and the CHI team are here whenever you are ready.
