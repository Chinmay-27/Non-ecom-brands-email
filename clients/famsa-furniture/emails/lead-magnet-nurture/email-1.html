How FAMSA financing works

No confusing fine print. Here’s the plain-language version.

Step 1

Check if you qualify — it’s free

Start with a free credit pre-qualification — online or in any of our 10 Texas stores. It takes just a few minutes and it doesn’t hurt your credit score to check. You’ll know quickly if financing is an option for you.

 

Step 2

Pick out what your home needs

Browse furniture, appliances, and mattresses in-store with a FAMSA team member who speaks your language — English or Spanish. Tell us which rooms you want to furnish and we’ll help you build the right package for your budget.

 

Step 3

Pay over time — in manageable monthly payments

Instead of one large payment upfront, you spread the cost across monthly installments — typically 6 to 24 months depending on what you choose. You go home with the furniture now. The payments fit into your regular budget.

 

Step 4

Your furniture is delivered to your door

Once approved, we schedule delivery — typically within a few days. Our crew brings everything in and sets it up. That empty room becomes a real living space your family can use right away.

Is FAMSA financing right for you?

Our in-house financing is built for Texas families who need real access — not a perfect credit score. It’s a popular choice for:

 
 First-time homeowners or renters furnishing a place from scratch
 
 Families rebuilding their credit who want a trusted lender in their corner
 
 Anyone who’d rather keep their savings intact and spread payments out
 
 New apartment residents who just moved and need to furnish fast

We welcome first-time applicants and credit rebuilders. Approval is based on your full application — everyone gets a fair review.
