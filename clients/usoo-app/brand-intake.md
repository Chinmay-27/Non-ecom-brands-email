# Brand Intake — USOO (usooapp.com)
*Researched 2026-06-04 | Status: Beta (launched 2026)*

---

## 1. The Offer

**What does the brand sell?**
A video speed dating app. Users join live, structured 5-minute face-to-face video calls with compatible singles. After each call, both parties independently tap Like or Pass. Mutual likes unlock messaging, in-app icebreaker games, media sharing, and social chat rooms. No swiping on profiles — chemistry is experienced directly on camera.

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**
Real romantic connection without the time-waste of swipe culture. "5 minutes of video tells you more than 50 text messages." The promise is: you'll *feel* something — or you won't — and you'll know fast.

**Price point & commitment:**
| Plan | Price | Key limits |
|---|---|---|
| Freemium | $0/mo | 1 speed date/day, no card required |
| Pro ⭐ Most Popular | $30/mo | 25 speed dates/day, 25 connections, messaging, icebreakers |
| Premium | $50/mo | Unlimited dates, unlimited connections, social rooms, games, analytics, priority support |

Recurring monthly subscription. Cancel anytime. US-based support.

**Vertical:** SaaS/tech — B2C consumer dating app (adult, 18+)

---

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:**
Singles aged roughly 22–38 who are burned out on Tinder/Bumble/Hinge and want authentic, low-stakes face-to-face connection *before* committing to a real date. Digitally native, comfortable on video, craving something that feels human.

**Secondary segments:**
- Swipe-fatigued millennials (28–38) who've "tried everything"
- Gen Z daters (22–27) who are video-native and bored of text-first apps
- People re-entering dating after a long-term relationship
- Early adopters drawn to the novelty of the speed-dating format

**Pain points they're in when they find USOO:**
- "I've been swiping for months and haven't had a single real conversation"
- Ghosting — matched, texted briefly, silence
- Catfishing / profile misrepresentation
- Algorithm fatigue — feels like a slot machine, not dating
- First dates are awkward with total strangers they know nothing about

**How they describe success:** "I finally felt a real spark." / "I could just *tell* in the first 2 minutes." / "No games, no filters — just us."

**Lead sources:** Social media (Instagram @usoo.llc, TikTok @usoollc, Facebook, X/Twitter); word of mouth from early beta users; app store discovery.

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:** Same day to days. Signup takes 2 minutes; free tier is immediately usable. Conversion to Pro/Premium happens when the 1-date/day free cap frustrates an engaged user.

**Free trial / first-session offer:** Yes — freemium tier is the trial. 1 video speed date per day, no credit card required. The cap is the natural conversion trigger.

**What closes the deal:** Self-serve upgrade at the in-app paywall (when free limit is hit) + email nurture sequence for free users who haven't upgraded. No sales call required.

**#1 objection / hesitation:**
Video anxiety — "I'm nervous to be on camera with a stranger." Secondary: "What if I don't match with anyone?" The format is novel; overcoming camera shyness and setting outcome expectations is the main pre-purchase job-to-be-done.

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:** First mutual match — both people tap Like after a 5-minute live video call and see the match confirmation. This is when the product clicks: *"Oh, this actually works."*

**How long does it take:** Could happen in the very first session (self-serve). The setup (profile + joining a session) takes under 5 minutes. First session could be same-day.

**Is it self-serve or assisted:** Fully self-serve. The queue system automatically pairs compatible users once they tap "Match & Video Now."

*This moment anchors Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding — get users to their first session and first match as fast as possible.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** Yes — monthly subscription (Pro $30, Premium $50).

**Renewal cycle:** Monthly. Free tier creates a daily habit loop (1 date/day) before conversion.

**Expansion path:** Freemium → Pro → Premium. Premium unlocks social chat rooms, post-match games & media sharing, and advanced analytics — positioned as "the complete USOO experience."

**Churn / drop-off risks:**
- No match in the first 1–3 sessions (expectation mismatch → abandonment)
- Video anxiety causes users to sign up but never join a session
- Post-match messaging stalls and the matched connection goes cold
- Free-tier users who never hit the cap naturally (low engagement = no conversion trigger)
- Lapsed paid subscribers who matched once but lost momentum

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal:** All (full lifecycle build from scratch — brand is in beta)

**Priority order:**
1. **Welcome & Onboarding** — get signups to their first session and first match; overcome video anxiety
2. **Freemium-to-Pro Conversion** — hit the daily cap → upgrade sequence
3. **Session Reminders & No-Show Recovery** — keep registered users showing up

**Brand voice / tone:**
Warm, playful, and emotionally direct. Leans into swipe-culture frustration with a knowing wink, then pivots to genuine optimism about real connection. Conversational and inclusive — never corporate or clinical. Encouraging without being cheesy. Think: a cool, emotionally intelligent friend who is genuinely rooting for you to find someone. Uses casual language ("vibe," "spark"), short sentences, and punchy CTAs.

Sample copy register: *"Swiping wasn't working. Neither was sending 'hey.' USOO gives you 5 minutes to feel something real — no filter, no profile game, just you."*

**Compliance constraints:**
- 18+ age verification required on all site entry
- 256-bit SSL encryption; end-to-end data protection stated on site
- Standard CAN-SPAM and GDPR (include unsubscribe, physical address)
- Adult dating platform — avoid guaranteeing romantic outcomes; include appropriate disclaimers
- Refund policy page exists — reference in billing/subscription emails

**Sending volume:** Unknown — beta-stage list. Start conservative; ramp with list growth.
