A thought

Businesses grow. Brands sometimes need to catch up.

In the time since we worked together, I'd guess your business has evolved — new services, new clients, maybe a different direction than when we first started. That's a good thing. But it also means your brand might be showing its age a little, even if you can't quite put your finger on why.

It's a pattern I see often: the logo and collateral that felt exactly right at launch start to feel slightly off once the business matures. Not broken — just not quite keeping pace. And that gap tends to quietly cost you credibility before you notice it.

Signs it might be time for a refresh

→ Your services or offering have changed since the original design
→ You're targeting a different or more premium audience now
→ New collateral needs have piled up (website, brochure, event materials)
→ Something about your current look quietly bothers you
