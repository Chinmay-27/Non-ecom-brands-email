Here's what they don't tell you

You've been told processing fees are just "the cost of doing business." Fixed. Unavoidable. Part of the deal.

That's true for almost every business cost — except this one.

Visa and Mastercard both permit compliant surcharging. That means a business owner can pass the processing cost to the card user — exactly the way your utility company passes along a fuel surcharge, or the way airlines display a credit card booking fee. It's disclosed at the point of sale, it's capped by card network rules, and when it's set up correctly, your net processing cost drops to effectively 0%.

That's not a gimmick. That's how Paymentum merchants stop watching thousands of dollars walk out the door every year.
