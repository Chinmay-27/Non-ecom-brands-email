What Think+ actually is

Think Energy is a licensed retail electricity provider operating across 13 deregulated US states. Energy Advisors — people like you — earn commissions by referring real households and small businesses to Think Energy's clean-electricity and community solar plans.

When a neighbor you referred switches their electricity plan, you earn an acquisition bonus. As long as they stay on the plan, you earn a monthly residual. That's it.

The product is electricity — not other advisors.

You don't stockpile product, buy a starter kit, or hit a monthly purchase quota. You refer people to a utility alternative they'd be paying for anyway. Your commission comes from Think Energy — not from recruiting fees.

How it's different from what you're probably picturing

✓ $99/year — no monthly fee, ever. One flat annual cost. No auto-ship, no minimum personal purchase, no hidden charges.
✓ No inventory to carry. Electricity isn't a physical product. Nothing to store in your garage, nobody pressuring you to "move units."
✓ No quotas. There's no monthly referral minimum to stay active or keep your residuals.
✓ Residuals tied to real customers, not recruitment. Your monthly commission exists only as long as the households you referred are actively on a Think Energy plan — not because you signed up someone below you.
