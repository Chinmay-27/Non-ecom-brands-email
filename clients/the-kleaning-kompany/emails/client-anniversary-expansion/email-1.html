Three ways to do even more for your facility

These are the services our existing clients add most often at the one-year mark — each one addresses something that routine cleaning alone can't handle.

★

Specialty Floor Stripping & Waxing

Daily mopping maintains the surface. Stripping and waxing restores it. We remove the buildup, apply a professional-grade finish, and your floors look the way they did on opening day.

✓

Deep Carpet Extraction

Vacuuming removes what's on top. Hot-water extraction pulls out what's embedded — allergens, odors, ground-in soil. The result is carpet that looks, smells, and performs like new.

Commercial Kitchen Cleaning

Grease traps, hood filters, floor drains, equipment surfaces — the areas inspectors check first. If you have a kitchen or break room that needs a deeper standard of clean, we can scope it separately or add it to your existing schedule.
