This Week's Affirmation

"I don't have to earn my place.
I was already built for this room."

Hey {{first_name}},

Read that again. Slower this time.

I wrote that affirmation at 11pm on a Tuesday when I was deep in my feelings — the kind of Tuesday where you're questioning everything and wondering if you're really built for the life you're trying to build. I almost didn't share it. It felt too raw.

But then I remembered something a mentor told me early on: the truth that scares you to say out loud is almost always the one someone else desperately needs to hear.

So here it is, sis. You don't have to perform, shrink, or over-explain yourself to justify being in the room. You belong there because you are H3r — and H3r doesn't wait for permission.

 

The Idea This Week

Stop Preparing to Be Ready. You Already Are.

There's this thing that happens to us — specifically to women who have had to work twice as hard just to be taken half as seriously. We develop what I call the "one more thing" loop: one more certification, one more year of experience, one more course, one more sign from the universe.

Meanwhile, someone else — often less qualified, less intentional, less everything — walks right into the opportunity you've been "getting ready for."

This week, I want you to try something small but powerful:

Write down one thing you've been waiting until you're "more ready" to do. Then ask yourself: what's the smallest version of that thing I could do this week? Not the perfect version. The messy, first-draft version.

Do that. Reply and tell me what it is. I read every single one.
