What to wear tomorrow

These four things will make a bigger difference in your final photos than almost anything else:

1

Solid colors win every time

Busy patterns, stripes, and small checks create visual noise that pulls the eye away from your face. A solid navy, charcoal, burgundy, or forest green puts the focus where it belongs — on you.

2

Bring a backup top

Pack one or two outfit options so we can shoot variety in a single session. Different looks give you more choices when you're reviewing your proofs — and it takes less than two minutes to change.

3

Skip the logos and branding

Visible brand logos date quickly and can come across as unprofessional in a headshot context. Go logo-free so your image stays timeless and works across platforms for years.

4

Get a good night's sleep

Rested eyes and skin make a genuine difference in portraits. A solid seven or eight hours tonight is the easiest thing you can do to look like the best version of yourself tomorrow morning.
