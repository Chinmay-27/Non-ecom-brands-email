The 5-Minute Confidence Reset

This week's practice is something I reach for every single Monday morning before I do anything else. It takes five minutes. It costs nothing. And it genuinely changes how you show up for the rest of the day.

Here's what to do:

Step 1 — Name one thing you did right last week.
Not something huge. One small win. A hard email you sent. A boundary you held. Showing up when you didn't feel like it. Write it down.

Step 2 — Read it out loud. Seriously.
Your brain needs to hear your voice say it. This isn't affirmation-poster fluff — there's real science behind speaking evidence of your own competence to yourself.

Step 3 — Set one intention for this week.
Not a to-do list. One feeling or quality you want to embody: "This week I'm showing up boldly." Write it somewhere you'll see it.

That's it. Five minutes. No app, no subscription, no special journal required — just you, a pen, and a few quiet moments.
