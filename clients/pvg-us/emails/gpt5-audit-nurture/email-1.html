The Audit Finding

AI readiness isn't a model problem. It's a data-unification problem. The gap your audit reveals isn't about which AI you choose — it's about whether your systems can feed it real, connected, trustworthy data.

A unified source of truth isn't a nice-to-have. It's the prerequisite.

Every reliable AI workflow — automated prior auth, predictive supply reorder, revenue cycle exception routing — runs on the assumption that the data underneath it is complete and current. The moment a surgeon preference card lives only in one system, or a billing modifier sits only in another, your AI can't be trusted to act on it.

That's not a workflow problem. It's an architecture problem — and it's exactly what enterprise interoperability solves before AI is layered on top.
