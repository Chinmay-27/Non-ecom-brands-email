So here's the honest answer.

The semantic layer sits between your systems — it doesn't replace them.

Most integration pain comes from point-to-point connections — every new integration is another brittle wire between two systems that weren't designed to talk. When one changes, everything breaks.

Paradigm's approach is different. We deploy a semantic data layer — a translation infrastructure that maps your EHR, ERP, and billing platform to a common data model. Each system keeps doing exactly what it does. The semantic layer reads, translates, and routes in real time without touching your source systems at the application level.

No rip-and-replace. No "our system or nothing." Your existing platforms stay in place — they just finally talk to each other.
