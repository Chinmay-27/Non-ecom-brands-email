## 1. The Offer

**What they sell:** A single hero product — "Wellness in a Bottle," a full-spectrum CBD tincture (163 mL, 3,300 mg CBD) blended with organic extra-virgin olive oil. Branded under "Healed by Farm," John Rankin's personal wellness brand. Founder also sells his book *Healed by Farm* (available on Amazon and through the site).

**Core outcome the customer is buying:** Drug-free, natural relief from chronic pain, inflammation, and disrupted sleep — without additives or fillers.

**Price point & commitment:** $99.95 per bottle, free shipping. One-time purchase only — no subscription, bundle, or auto-ship option currently exists. Single SKU storefront.

**Vertical:** Health & Wellness — DTC CBD / Natural Supplement (Other)

---

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:** Health-conscious adults aged roughly 35–65+ experiencing chronic joint pain, back pain, arthritis, or sleep disruption. Drawn to natural/ancestral health approaches; skeptical of pharmaceuticals or synthetic supplements. Typically introduced through search, social media, or word-of-mouth in the natural wellness space.

**Secondary segments:**
- Sleep-focused buyers (pain-adjacent; better sleep is a frequently cited outcome)
- Natural health advocates who follow ancestral nutrition content (book/podcast audience)
- Wholesale / retail buyers (the site has a "Interested in carrying Healed by Farm?" inquiry form — B2B segment exists but is separate)

**Problem they're in when they start looking:** "I've tried prescriptions or OTC painkillers and either don't want the side effects or they stopped working. I want something natural that actually works."

**Lead sources (inferred):** Primarily organic search (CBD/pain relief keywords), content marketing (podcast, book), and likely some paid social. The site reads as content-first / authority-driven rather than pure ad-spend.

**How they describe success:** "I can finally sleep through the night." / "My knee pain is manageable again." / "I don't have to reach for Advil every morning."

---

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time to purchase:** Days to 2–3 weeks. The ~$100 price point and CBD-category skepticism require trust-building before checkout. Podcast and book content serve as the slow-burn top-of-funnel.

**Free trial / entry offer:** None. No sample, no trial size, no free consult. The book (*Healed by Farm*) and podcast function as soft trust anchors but don't gate an email address in an obvious way.

**Who/what closes the deal:** Self-serve add-to-cart. Testimonials and the founder's personal story do the heavy lifting. No salesperson or booked call involved.

**#1 objection before buying:** *"Will this actually work for me, or is this just another overhyped CBD product?"* — Quality/efficacy skepticism is endemic to the CBD category. The "no chemicals, farm-sourced" angle and John's story are the primary objection-handlers.

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:** Consistent daily use of the tincture resulting in a first noticeable improvement — typically reduced pain intensity or improved sleep — within **7–14 days**.

**Self-serve or assisted:** Entirely self-serve. There is no onboarding call, dosing consult, or follow-up sequence detected. This is a major gap: buyers who don't know the right dose, timing, or what to expect will drop off before experiencing the benefit.

*This makes Welcome & Onboarding the highest-priority campaign — it needs to set dosing expectations, normalize the timeline, and keep the buyer engaged through the first two weeks.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** No — revenue is purely transactional. There is no subscription, membership, or auto-ship option. Each re-order requires the customer to remember and return voluntarily.

**Renewal / repeat cycle:** A 163 mL bottle at a typical CBD serving is roughly a 30–45 day supply. Re-order window is around day 25–35.

**Expansion opportunities:**
- Auto-ship / subscription program (not yet offered — significant revenue unlock)
- Multi-bottle bundles (save-on-bulk)
- Book upsell (already exists but likely not systematically emailed)
- Wholesale partnerships (B2B expansion path already indicated on site)

**Churn / drop-off problem:** High by default — no subscription, no loyalty program, no detected re-order sequence. Silent churn is the primary retention risk. Customers who experience benefit but don't get a re-order prompt at the right moment simply don't return.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal (inferred):** More conversions and better retention. The single SKU / no-subscription model means every dollar of growth comes from new buyers or re-orders — both need email infrastructure.

**Target metric:** Re-order rate (repeat purchase within 60 days of first purchase) and post-purchase engagement (CTR on onboarding sequence).

**Brand voice / tone:** Warm, plain-spoken, grounded in founder John Rankin's personal health journey. Farm authenticity over clinical language. Educational but not preachy. No hype. Ancestral-health lens — "simple, farm-based" is the north star phrase.

**Compliance constraints:**
- CBD health supplement: FDA disclaimer required on all emails making wellness claims
- No disease-treatment language ("treats arthritis" → ✗; "supports joint comfort" → ✓)
- Products contain <0.3% THC — state-level legality is buyer's responsibility
- CAN-SPAM compliance required
- No HIPAA exposure

**Sending volume:** Unknown — start conservatively given list size is unconfirmed.

---

## Output: First 3 Campaigns to Build

1. **Welcome & Onboarding** — Set dosing expectations, explain the timeline to first effect (7–14 days), share John's story, and keep the new buyer engaged until they hit the aha moment. This is the #1 priority; without it, churn before benefit is nearly guaranteed.

2. **Re-order / Renewal Nudge** — Trigger at day 25–28 post-purchase to prompt a refill before the bottle runs out. Include a "how are you feeling?" check-in and social proof from customers who saw results at the 30-day mark. (Substitutes for Campaign 6 — no subscription to renew, but re-order cadence is equivalent.)

3. **Lead-Magnet Nurture** — Capture the book/podcast audience into an email sequence that educates on CBD, ancestral wellness, and natural pain relief — and eventually converts to a first purchase. This closes the top-of-funnel leak from existing content assets.
