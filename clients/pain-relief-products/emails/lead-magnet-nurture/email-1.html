The problem no one talks about

On one side, you have prescription painkillers and NSAIDs. They work — until they don't, or until your stomach lining, liver, or blood pressure starts sending signals you can't ignore.

On the other side, you have the CBD market. And if you've looked into it at all, you know it's a mess. Gummies loaded with corn syrup and artificial colors. Tinctures cut with cheap carrier oils. Labels that say one thing and contain another. Products made by people who have never set foot on a farm, selling the idea of "natural" wellness without any of the substance behind it.

So most people end up stuck. The pill bottle feels wrong, but the alternative feels untrustworthy. I lived in that gap for a long time.

What I did differently

I went back to basics. I looked at what our grandparents used before the pharmacy on every corner. I talked to farmers. I read everything I could find about how hemp actually works in the body — the full-spectrum effect, the role of terpenes and cannabinoids working together, why a whole-plant extract is fundamentally different from an isolate.

And then I built something simple: a full-spectrum CBD tincture with exactly two ingredients — farm-sourced hemp extract and organic extra-virgin olive oil. No additives. No fillers. No gimmicks. The same kind of honest, from-the-land approach that our ancestors used to take care of themselves before it got complicated.

I call it Wellness in a Bottle. And the book below is where I tell the full story — how I got there, what I learned along the way, and why I believe this approach works when so much else doesn't.
