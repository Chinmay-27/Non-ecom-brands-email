Why Zillow’s estimate is just a starting point

Zillow, Redfin, and every other listing site pull from the same publicly recorded data. They don’t know that your kitchen was just remodeled, that the school district line shifted two years ago, or that three homes on your street went under contract in under a week last month.

That local context is exactly what separates a home that sits on the market for 60 days from one that gets multiple offers in the first weekend.

We’re not saying the estimate above is wrong. We’re saying it’s incomplete — and in a competitive market like Southern Maryland, the difference between a good price and the right price can mean tens of thousands of dollars staying in your pocket.
