What you'll notice when it arrives

01

Check the roast date on the bag. It'll say today's date (or tomorrow's, depending on cutoff). That's not a printed label — it's when your coffee was actually made.

02

Smell it before you grind. Fresh-roasted coffee off-gasses CO₂ for the first week. You'll notice the aroma the second you break the seal — denser, darker, more complex than anything that's been sitting.

03

Watch the bloom. When you add hot water, fresh coffee blooms — it bubbles and rises because of the CO₂ still trapped inside the beans. Stale coffee sits flat. If yours blooms, the coffee is doing what it's supposed to.
