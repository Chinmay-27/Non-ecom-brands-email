## 1. The Offer

**What they sell:** Functional wellness supplements across five product families — Awaken (energy/cognition), Detox, Nourish, Restore, and Kids — sold via the Vital Health Global platform. The business also offers an affiliate/network marketing income opportunity.

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**
- *Consumer*: Sustained daily energy, mental clarity, and whole-body vitality through science-based natural formulas.
- *Business prospect*: Financial freedom and residual income by building a network of wellness customers and affiliates.

**Price point & commitment:**
- Individual products: $18–$100 per item
- Annual membership: $29.99/yr (referral-only; unlocks member-only pricing and perks)
- Vital Health Scanner: $149 (with Elite Pack) or $300 standalone
- Bundles/packs: Available at discounted rates (e.g., Elite Pack)
- No mandatory autoship identified; purchases can be one-time or repeated

**Vertical:** Health & Wellness / Network Marketing (MLM-adjacent, consumer-product-first model)

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:** Health-conscious adults 25–50, skewing female, who are frustrated with persistent low energy, brain fog, or poor gut health and are actively seeking natural supplement solutions. They've likely tried drugstore brands without strong results and are open to premium, science-backed alternatives if a trusted person recommends them.

**Secondary segments:**
1. **Affiliate/entrepreneur prospects** — entrepreneurially minded individuals (often already in the ICP's social circle) who are interested in a side income or home business. They buy products first to validate before promoting.
2. **Existing members** — already past the referral + membership step; need product activation, cross-sell into other families, and upsell to Scanner or higher-tier bundles.

**Problem they're in when they start looking:**
- Chronic fatigue that coffee can't fix
- Struggling with focus, mood, or metabolism
- Looking for a trusted recommendation from someone they know (referral-driven discovery)
- Seeking a flexible income stream (business segment)

**Lead sources:** Almost entirely referral-driven (the affiliate model is the distribution engine); social media content by the distributor; virtual events and webinars.

**How they describe success:** "I have energy again," "I actually feel the difference," "I'm finally consistent with my health," "I've built something of my own."

---

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:**
- Product purchase: Same day to ~1 week (referral-driven, impulse-friendly price points)
- Business opportunity: 2–8 weeks (requires presentations, follow-up, relationship-building)

**Free trial / first offer:** No free trial. The $29.99/yr membership is the lowest formal barrier to entry (unlocks member pricing). Informal samples may be distributed by the distributor in person. The Vital Health Scanner demo can serve as an in-person hook.

**Who/what closes the deal:** The affiliate/distributor closes via personal conversation, social content, or a group presentation. Checkout is then self-serve through the referral link.

**#1 objection before buying:**
- *Product buyer*: "Is this worth the price vs. what I can get at Costco/Amazon?"
- *Business prospect*: "Is this a pyramid scheme / MLM? Will I actually make money?"

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:** The subscriber's first noticeable, felt improvement — more energy in the morning, better focus during work, or a positive physical change — typically surfacing within 7–14 days of consistent daily product use.

**How long / self-serve or assisted:** Self-serve once products are in hand, but it requires proper onboarding: knowing *which* product to start with, *when* to take it, and *what* to expect in the first 2–4 weeks. Without this guidance, customers abandon before the aha moment arrives.

*This is the anchor for Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** Yes — annual membership renewal ($29.99/yr) and repeat product purchases once a product becomes part of the customer's daily routine. The affiliate model also creates structural stickiness: distributors must stay active to maintain commissions.

**Renewal / repeat cycle:** Annual membership; product repurchase cycle approximately 30–60 days depending on product type.

**What "expansion" looks like:**
- Upgrading from single products to bundles or the Elite Pack
- Adding the Vital Health Scanner ($149–$300)
- Cross-selling into additional product families (e.g., consumer in Awaken family adds Detox)
- Business expansion: advancing affiliate rank, building a downline, attending live events

**Churn / no-show / drop-off problem:**
- Product users who don't feel results in the first 30 days and quietly stop reordering
- Affiliates who sign up for the business opportunity but never recruit a second person
- Membership renewals that go unnoticed (passive billing, low engagement before renewal date)
- Leads who attended an opportunity presentation but never followed up

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal (inferred):** More conversions/bookings (turning referred leads into buyers and business-opportunity prospects into active affiliates) + better retention (reducing early product drop-off)

**Target metric (inferred):** Product reorder rate; affiliate activation rate (% who recruit at least one person); membership renewal rate

**Brand voice / tone:** Warm, empowering, aspirational. Science-backed but personal — leads with transformation stories, not ingredient lists. Community-oriented language ("family," "journey," "legacy"). Conversational. More formal/business-like for opportunity messaging; more personal/relatable for wellness consumer messaging. No hype. No MLM clichés ("be your own boss," "unlimited income").

**Compliance constraints:**
- **FTC**: Income claims require disclosure statement in all affiliate/business-opportunity emails
- **FDA**: No disease treatment/cure/prevention claims for supplement products; must use "supports," "may help," etc.
- **CAN-SPAM / CASL**: Standard unsubscribe + sender ID requirements
- **GDPR**: Apply if list includes EU contacts

**Sending volume:** Unknown — depends on list size; start conservatively.
