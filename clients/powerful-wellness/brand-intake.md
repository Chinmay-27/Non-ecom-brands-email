## 1. The offer

**What they sell:**
- **Free 15-minute discovery call** — the primary top-of-funnel conversion event ("no pressure, see if we're a fit")
- **1-on-1 coaching** — personalized plan, direct messaging between sessions, clinical/NP-informed approach (listed as "MOST POPULAR")
- **6-week coaching program** — structured reset across the "5 Pillars of Health": Medications/Medical (GLP-1, LIPOLEAN, Peptides), Fitness, Nutrition, and Community Support
- **Digital products / ebooks** — sold through a Wellness Store (titles and prices not publicly listed)
- **Free checklist** — "important information before starting any health/fitness/nutrition program" (lead magnet)

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**
> *"Lose weight, become a better version of yourself. Reclaim your energy and balance your hormones."*
> Primary hooks: hormonal balance, weight loss (esp. metabolic/GLP-1), energy, and aging well.

**Key differentiator:**
Founder has **nurse practitioner (NP) credentials** — clinical expertise embedded in coaching, including access to prescription-adjacent interventions (GLP-1, peptides, LIPOLEAN). This is a meaningful trust signal in a crowded wellness-coaching market and should be foregrounded in every email.

**Price point:** Not publicly disclosed. Likely $500–$3,000+ range for coaching packages based on comparable NP-led health coaching programs. Requires a discovery call to get pricing.

**Commitment type:** Project-based (6 weeks) + ongoing 1:1 retainer-style coaching.

**Vertical:** Health & wellness coaching / telehealth-adjacent — **closest playbook vertical: B2B/professional services** (high trust, clinical credibility, longer consideration cycle) crossed with **local/service** (appointment-driven, relationship sale).

## 2. The audience

**Primary ICP:**
Women 35–50+ who feel their body has "changed on them" — fatigue, weight gain, hormonal shifts (perimenopause/menopause), and frustration that what worked before doesn't anymore. The site voice: *"You've spent years caring for others. Now it's time to care for YOU."*

**Pain-state when they start looking:**
- Exhausted despite sleeping enough
- Unexplained weight gain, especially midsection
- Navigating perimenopause/menopause without clear guidance
- Tried diets/gym — didn't stick or didn't work at this life stage
- Skeptical of generic wellness advice; want someone with clinical credibility

**Where leads come from (inferred):**
Organic search, social (likely Instagram/Facebook — the 35–55 women demographic), word of mouth, and the free email content list. Blog is early-stage (only 2 posts found).

**Secondary segments worth separating:**
- Women interested in medical weight loss (GLP-1/peptides path) — higher urgency, higher ticket
- Women who want lifestyle-only coaching (fitness + nutrition pillar, no Rx)
- Women actively in perimenopause/menopause — hormonal education content resonates

## 3. The sales cycle

**Time from first contact → paid client:** Likely **1–4 weeks** — lead downloads checklist or signs up for free content → nurture → books free 15-min call → call converts to coaching proposal → enrollment.

**The "trial":** The **free 15-minute discovery call** IS the trial/consult moment. This is the single conversion event the entire email funnel must drive toward and protect.

**Who/what closes the deal:** The discovery call itself. The founder/practitioner is the closer. Email's job is to (a) get the call booked and (b) ensure the prospect shows up prepared, excited, and pre-sold.

**#1 objection before buying:** *"Is this just another wellness program? Will it actually work for someone my age/situation?"* → Counter with NP credibility, clinical approach, personalization, and specific social proof from women like them.

---

## 4. The "aha" / first-value moment

**For leads (pre-purchase):** Completing the free 15-min discovery call and leaving with one actionable step + a clear picture of what coaching looks like. The call is the aha.

**For new clients (post-purchase):** First coaching call + receiving their personalized plan. The moment they feel *"this is built for me, not a generic program."*

**Implication for Campaign 1 (Welcome & Onboarding):**
- For **email list subscribers**: drive to book the free 15-min call.
- For **new paying clients**: confirm their enrollment and anchor them on the personalized-plan delivery moment.
- These are two separate welcome sequences (see Campaign Specs below).

## 5. Retention & recurring revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** Partially — 6-week program is project-based, but 1:1 coaching likely continues month-to-month afterward. No public membership/subscription structure.

**Repeat cycle:** After the 6-week program, clients likely continue as 1:1 coaching clients or re-enroll. The expansion path = moving from 6-week program → ongoing 1:1 retainer.

**Expansion:** 
- 6-week graduate → ongoing monthly 1:1 coaching
- Medical/metabolic track upsell (GLP-1, LIPOLEAN, peptides consultations)
- Ebook/digital products as lower-ticket touchpoints

**Churn/drop-off risk:** Wellness coaching dropout is real. Clients lose motivation around weeks 3–4. The onboarding and in-program email sequence needs to re-activate and reinforce commitment.

## 8. Compliance & tone

**Compliance flag — HEALTH CLAIMS:** Because the founder is a nurse practitioner and the offer includes clinical interventions (GLP-1, peptides, LIPOLEAN), emails must:
- Not make diagnostic claims ("will cure / treat / prevent")
- Use outcomes framing: "clients who work with [Name] report..." / "designed to help you..."
- Include a standard health disclaimer in footers (especially for the metabolic/medical track)
- Be reviewed against FTC health-claim guidelines and state NP scope-of-practice rules

**Brand voice (inferred from site copy):**
- Warm, direct, empowering — woman-to-woman
- Medically credible but not clinical/cold
- Permission-based ("when you're ready") — not pushy
- Key phrases to echo: "years caring for others / now care for YOU," "better version of yourself," "vibrant aging"

## Output: First 3 campaigns to build

Based on gaps in §7 and the brand's funnel (everything runs through the discovery call):

1. **Campaign 4 — Booking, Reminders & No-Show Recovery** ← build first. The discovery call IS the revenue event. A branded 3-email confirmation + reminder sequence + 2-email no-show recovery = immediate, measurable impact. Fastest win.

2. **Campaign 1 — Welcome & Onboarding (List subscriber branch)** ← email list is the primary asset. 5 emails, ~14 days, driving to book the free call. This is the always-on workhorse.

3. **Campaign 2 — Lead-Magnet Nurture (Checklist download)** ← the checklist is already a lead magnet. A 5-email, 50-day sequence from checklist download → discovery call booking turns a passive download into a sales conversation.

*Campaigns 3 (post-call conversion), 5 (newsletter), and 7 (win-back) follow in that order.*
