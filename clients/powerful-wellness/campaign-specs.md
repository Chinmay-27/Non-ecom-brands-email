# Campaign Specs — Powerful Wellness
*Priority build order: Campaign 4 → Campaign 1 → Campaign 2 → Campaign 3 → Campaign 5 → Campaign 7*

---

## Why this order

Powerful Wellness runs entirely on a single conversion event: **the free 15-minute discovery call**. Every email we build either gets someone to book that call, ensures they show up, or closes them after it. So we work backward from the call:

1. **Protect the call** (Campaign 4 — Reminders)
2. **Fill the call calendar** (Campaign 1 — Welcome / list subscribers)
3. **Warm cold leads toward booking** (Campaign 2 — Nurture / checklist downloads)
4. **Close after the call** (Campaign 3 — Post-consult conversion)
5. **Stay top-of-mind long-term** (Campaign 5 — Newsletter)
6. **Recover the ones who went cold** (Campaign 7 — Win-back)

---

## Campaign 4 — Booking, Reminders & No-Show Recovery
*(Build this first — fastest direct revenue impact)*

**Objective:** Maximize attended discovery calls. Recover no-shows. Progress attended calls to enrollment.

**Trigger:** Discovery-call booking confirmed (via Calendly or scheduling tool).
**Segment:** Anyone who books the free 15-min call.

**Tone note:** Warm and professional — like a trusted health practitioner's front desk, not a sales bot. Reference the founder's NP credentials to reinforce that this is a clinical-quality conversation, not a pushy sales call.

## Campaign 1 — Welcome & Onboarding (List subscriber branch)
*(Build second — the always-on workhorse filling the call calendar)*

**Objective:** Convert a new email subscriber into a booked discovery call within 14 days.

**Trigger:** Email list signup (via homepage form or free-content opt-in).
**Segment:** New subscribers. Branch by intake question if added to form: "biggest challenge" (energy / weight / hormones / sleep).

**Tone:** Personal, like the founder is writing herself. Not a newsletter blast — a real email from a real practitioner.

---

**Email 1 — Instant: Warm welcome + deliver the quick win**
> **Subject:** Welcome — and your first step toward feeling like yourself again
> **Preview:** One thing to do in the next 5 minutes.
>
> Hi [First name],
>
> Welcome — I'm really glad you're here.
>
> I'm [Founder Name], a nurse practitioner and the founder of Powerful Wellness. I created this community for one reason: I kept seeing women in their 40s and 50s — smart, capable, caring women — who had spent years looking after everyone else and ended up exhausted, frustrated, and disconnected from their own bodies.
>
> You deserve better than that. And the right support makes all the difference.
>
> **Your first step:** Download your free checklist — the things worth knowing before starting any health or wellness program. It'll take 5 minutes and save you from the most common mistakes I see.
>
> **→ [Download the checklist]**
>
> Over the next couple of weeks I'll share the most useful things I know about hormones, metabolism, and energy for women at this stage of life. Real clinical insight, practical and jargon-free.
>
> And if you ever want to talk through where you are specifically — not generic advice, but *your* situation — I offer a free 15-minute call. No pressure. Just a conversation.
>
> So glad you're here,
> [Founder Name], NP

---

**Email 2 — Day 1: The "aha" — the clinical edge**
> **Subject:** Why what worked at 35 doesn't work at 45 (it's not willpower)
>
> *Content angle:* Explain in plain language what happens to metabolism, hormones, and body composition in perimenopause/mid-life. Position NP expertise: "this is what I see in practice." One key insight. One soft CTA to read a related piece or book a call.

---

**Email 3 — Day 3: Social proof + handle the "will this work for me?" objection**
> **Subject:** "I'd tried everything. Then I tried this."
>
> *Content angle:* Feature a client story (Karen, Tiffany, or Melissa from the testimonials). Frame it: woman her age, similar frustration, specific result. Close with: "Every plan I build is personalized — book a free call to find out what yours looks like."

---

**Email 4 — Day 7: Education — the 5 Pillars framework**
> **Subject:** The 5 things I look at when someone's stuck
>
> *Content angle:* Introduce the 5 Pillars framework briefly (Medical, Fitness, Nutrition, Sleep, Community). Position this as the brand's proprietary clinical lens. Make the reader feel like they're getting inside-information. CTA: "In our free 15-min call I walk through which pillars are most affecting you."

---

**Email 5 — Day 12: Human nudge + low-friction CTA**
> **Subject:** Quick question, [First name]
>
> Hi [First name],
>
> I've been thinking about you since you joined.
>
> You came here because something isn't quite right — energy, weight, hormones, sleep. And I just want to check: are you getting what you came for?
>
> If you're ready to figure out what's actually going on (not just tips, but a real plan for *you*), a free 15-minute call is the fastest way to get there.
>
> **→ [Book your free call — no pressure]**
>
> And if you're still in "just exploring" mode, that's completely fine too. Keep reading — I'll keep sharing.
>
> [Founder Name]

**Behavioral branch:** Subscriber books the call → immediately exit this sequence and enter Campaign 4 (Booking & Reminders). Remove from further Welcome emails.

**Segmentation note:** If intake question is added to signup form, branch Email 2 and Email 3 by challenge:
- Energy/fatigue → hormone + adrenal fatigue content
- Weight → metabolic/GLP-1 angle (the clinical differentiator)
- Hormones/menopause → perimenopause content
- Sleep → sleep-hormone connection content

**Success metrics:** Call booking rate from this sequence, CTR on Email 3 (social proof) and Email 5 (CTA), reply rate.

## Campaign 2 — Lead-Magnet Nurture (Checklist download)
*(Build third — warms cold leads over 50 days toward booking)*

**Objective:** Move a checklist downloader from passive interest to booked discovery call.

**Trigger:** Checklist downloaded (if tracked separately from general email signup — ideally a distinct opt-in form).
**Segment:** Leads who have the checklist but haven't booked a call.

**Cadence:** 5 emails, ~10 days apart, mapped to the buyer journey.

| # | Day | Buyer stage | Content angle | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | Deliver + frame | Deliver checklist. Frame the *problem* it solves: "most women starting a wellness program make one of these 3 mistakes — here's how to avoid them" | Download / read the checklist |
| 2 | 10 | Education | "How to actually lose weight after 40 (it's not what you think)" — explain metabolic shifts, NP lens | Read / save |
| 3 | 20 | Social proof | Client transformation story. Specific: challenge → approach → outcome. "This is what a personalized plan looks like" | Book a free call to see your plan |
| 4 | 35 | Objection handling | Address "I've tried programs before and they didn't work." The answer: generic programs aren't built for your hormones, your stage of life, your body. Clinical personalization is different. | Book a free call |
| 5 | 50 | Decision | "When you're ready, here's how we start." Recap the free call. Low friction, no pressure. | Book your free 15-min call |

**Key copy angle running through all 5:** The NP credential is the throughline. Every email earns trust by leading with clinical insight — not motivational copy, not generic wellness tips. The reader should feel: *"This person actually knows what's happening in my body."*

**Success metrics:** CTR on email 3 (social proof), email 4 (objection), call-booking rate by day 50, lead-score progression.

## Campaign 3 — Post-Consult Conversion
*(Build fourth — closes the deal after the call)*

**Objective:** Convert an attended discovery call into a paid coaching enrollment.

**Trigger:** Discovery call attended (Campaign 4, Part C already starts this — Campaigns 3 and 4-C overlap; treat C1/C2 of Campaign 4 as the opening emails here, then continue below if no enrollment).

**Segment:** Call attended, no enrollment within 72 hours.

| # | Timing | Content | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Same day | Warm recap + next step (= Campaign 4, Email C1) | Review options / proposal |
| 2 | Day 3 | Soft follow-up (= Campaign 4, Email C2) | Reply / review |
| 3 | Day 7 | Case study — a client who was in the same place, made the decision, outcome | Book / enroll |
| 4 | Day 14 | "Still thinking it over?" — address the #1 hesitation (cost? time? "is this for someone like me?") | Reply with your question / book |
| 5 | Day 21 | Honest close — "I only take a limited number of clients at a time. If now isn't the right moment, no pressure — but I'd hate for you to lose your spot." | Enroll or let me know |

**Compliance note:** Do not use artificial scarcity. Only reference limited availability if it is factually true (NP practice genuinely has finite client capacity — likely is true, so this is fine).

**Success metrics:** Call-to-enrollment conversion rate, time-to-close, which email in the sequence triggered the enrollment click.

## Campaign 5 — Value Newsletter
*(Build fifth — long-term authority and list health)*

**Objective:** Stay top-of-mind, build clinical authority, and softly re-engage the list every 2 weeks.

**Cadence:** Biweekly (every 2 weeks) — achievable with the current blog/content stage.

**Repeatable format:**
- **One clinical insight** (the NP angle — something a regular wellness blogger can't say)
- **One practical tip or protocol** (actionable, specific, women 40+ relevant)
- **One story** (client, community member, or the founder's own experience)
- **One soft CTA** (book a call, read more, reply with a question)

**Content pillar rotation:** Medical/hormones → Fitness → Nutrition → Sleep/recovery → Community/mindset → repeat.

**Subject-line angles:**
- "The hormone you're not thinking about (but should be)"
- "What I tell every new client in week one"
- "Why 8 hours of sleep isn't enough if you're doing this"
- "3 signs your metabolism needs clinical support, not just a diet"
- "[Client name] lost 18 lbs in 6 weeks — here's what we actually changed"

**Segment:** Full engaged list; suppress anyone currently in active onboarding (Campaign 1) or a live coaching program.

**Success metrics:** CTR, CTOR, reply rate, call bookings attributed to newsletter.

## Campaign 7 — Win-Back / Re-Engagement
*(Build sixth — list hygiene + revenue recovery)*

**Objective:** Reactivate dormant subscribers. Clean those who don't re-engage.

**Trigger:** No opens/clicks in 60–90 days (or no call booked in 90 days for list subscribers).

**4 emails over 3 weeks:**

| # | Day | Angle | Subject |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | "We miss you" + remind of value | "Still thinking about [energy/weight/hormones]?" |
| 2 | 7 | Fresh reason to return — new content, new program, or a seasonal hook | "Here's what's changed since you last checked in" |
| 3 | 14 | Low-friction offer — free call, free checklist | "Still free: your 15-minute call with [Founder Name]" |
| 4 | 21 | Sunset — honest and warm | "Should I stop sending these?" |

**Sunset Email (Email 4):**
> Subject: Should I stop sending these?
>
> Hi [First name],
>
> I've noticed you haven't opened my emails in a while — and that's completely okay. Life gets busy, priorities shift.
>
> I just want to make sure I'm only in your inbox if it's useful.
>
> **→ [Yes, keep me on the list]** — I'll keep sharing my best clinical insights for women's health.
>
> If I don't hear from you in the next few days, I'll remove you from my list — no hard feelings, and you can always come back.
>
> Wishing you well either way,
> [Founder Name]

**Success metrics:** Reactivation rate (click or rebook), list size post-sunset, deliverability improvement.

## Shared guidelines across all campaigns

**From-name:** [Founder Name] at Powerful Wellness (not just "Powerful Wellness" — personal sender name dramatically improves open and reply rates in coaching)

**Reply-to:** A real inbox the founder or a team member monitors. Every campaign references "just reply" — it must be true.

**Footer health disclaimer:** *"[Founder Name] is a licensed nurse practitioner. Information in these emails is educational and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your health regimen."*

**Unsubscribe language:** Warm, not legal-boilerplate: "Not the right fit anymore? Unsubscribe here — no hard feelings."

**Deliverability:** Send from a dedicated sending subdomain (e.g., mail.powerful-wellness.com). Authenticate with SPF, DKIM, DMARC before launching. Warm the list slowly if starting from scratch.
