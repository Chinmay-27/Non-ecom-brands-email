You’ve already tried. And it didn’t work.

Most of the women I work with have a history that sounds like this: cut carbs, ramped up cardio, tried intermittent fasting, downloaded an app, maybe hired a personal trainer. Something worked — briefly — and then stopped. Or never worked at all.

That’s not a willpower problem. That’s a biology problem.

After 35, your hormones shift in ways that fundamentally change how your body stores fat, uses energy, builds muscle, and regulates appetite. What worked at 28 genuinely does not work the same way at 42 — not because you’re doing it wrong, but because your body is operating on a different set of rules.

Generic programs don’t account for that. Ours is built around it.
