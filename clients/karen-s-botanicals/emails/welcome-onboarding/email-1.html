A story rooted in the old country — and in Georgia soil.

Long before "clean beauty" was a marketing phrase, Karen Palcho's Slovak grandmother was already doing it — pressing herbs, infusing oils, and blending balms from a handwritten recipe book that had passed through generations. When Karen founded this brand in 2004, she brought those 19th-century formulations back to life, exactly as written.

In 2011, Edward and Patricia Mitchell fell in love with those same recipes and made a quiet decision: to continue the work on their own family farm in Taylorsville, Georgia. Today, every product you receive was made in small batches right there — grown, harvested, or sourced with the same care your great-grandmother's kitchen demanded.

No synthetic fillers. No parabens. No ingredients you'd need a chemistry degree to pronounce. Just plants, and what they know how to do.
