This weekend in Baltimore

Patterson Park — Free Outdoor Play & Exploration

Patterson Park is 137 acres of Baltimore's best-kept secret — playgrounds, paddle boats, a pagoda to climb, and wide open green space where the kids can just run. It's free, it's stroller-friendly, and it's one of those places where you end up staying two hours longer than you planned. 🌳

📍 Where: 27 S. Patterson Park Ave, Baltimore, MD 21231

💰 Cost: Free

👶 Ages: All ages — toddlers to tweens, everyone wins

🌂 Rainy day? Swap it for the Maryland Science Center on Key Highway — kids are obsessed with the dinosaurs
