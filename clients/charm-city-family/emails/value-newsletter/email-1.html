🆕 This Weekend's Picks

🎉

Pick #1 — FREE

Patterson Park Family Festival

Free admission, live local music, lawn games, and a kids' activity village right in the park. This one's completely stroller-friendly — wide paved paths throughout — and the vibe is always easy-going and mixed. Families from all over the city come out, and it genuinely feels like Baltimore at its best.

FREE 🚪 Stroller-Friendly All Families Welcome

📅 Saturday, June 7 · 10am–4pm
📍 Patterson Park, 2600 E Baltimore St

⛈️

Pick #2 — Rainy-Day Backup

Maryland Science Center Drop-In Weekend

If Saturday's forecast has you side-eyeing the clouds, this is the move. Drop-in hands-on stations all weekend, plus the planetarium show that my own kids have asked to see three times running. Best for ages 3+ but even toddlers enjoy the dino section. Inner Harbor parking tip: check SpotHero ahead — Saturday mornings fill up fast.

🌧 Indoor Low-Cost w/ EBT Ages 2+

📅 Sat – Sun, June 7–8 · 10am–5pm
📍 601 Light St, Inner Harbor · EBT cardholders: $3/person via Museums for All

💎

Pick #3 — Hidden Gem

Govans Arboretum Nature Walk

This one comes straight from the group — a Charm City Family member posted about it last Sunday and said it's her family's new Sunday morning tradition. A quiet tucked-away nature trail in North Baltimore that most people drive right past. Trail is mostly flat and fine for little legs, and there's a small pond that had the kids obsessed for a solid 30 minutes.

FREE 🌿 Outdoors Quiet & Calm

📅 Open daily, sunrise to sunset
📍 5105 York Rd, Govans neighborhood — free street parking
