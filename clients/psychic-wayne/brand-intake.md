## 1. The Offer

**What does the brand sell?**  
Appointments (phone/online) for tarot card readings in five session lengths: 10, 15, 20, 30, and 60 minutes. Also sells digital astrology reports (natal chart, numerology, compatibility, transit), daily horoscope content, and tarot products (planners, e-books) via the website and Etsy shop.

**Core outcome the customer is buying:**  
Clarity and confident direction — specifically answers to questions about love, relationships, career, and major life decisions. The result is emotional resolution: knowing what to do next.

**Price point & commitment:**  
One-off session bookings; no mandatory subscription or retainer. Exact prices are not publicly listed on the site. Market-comparable range is roughly $25–$60 for 10–20 min sessions and $80–$150+ for 30–60 min sessions. Clients rebook at will.

**Vertical:**  
☑ Local & service (spiritual services, online-enabled)

## 2. The Audience

**Primary customer / ICP:**  
Women 25–55 in the US, spiritually curious or actively engaged with astrology/tarot content on social media. Seeks personalized spiritual guidance on love, relationships, or a major life crossroads. Likely follows astrology accounts on Instagram or TikTok and has tried free readings before.

**Secondary segments worth separating:**
1. **Returning believers** — past clients who trust Wayne and rebook periodically for life-update check-ins.
2. **Astrology/tarot enthusiasts** — consume free daily horoscope content on-site; prime upsell target for paid reports.
3. **Gift buyers** — purchasing a reading as a gift for a friend or family member.

**Problem they're in when they start looking:**  
Relationship uncertainty (should I stay or go?), career indecision, feeling stuck or unmoored, or a specific question they can't answer with logic alone. Often triggered by a life event: a breakup, a job change, a pregnancy, a loss.

**Where leads come from today:**  
Organic social (Instagram @psychicwayne1, Facebook @PsychicWayne, X/Twitter @PsychicWayne1), Google search (psychic readings Southern California, online tarot), VIP SMS list, and word of mouth from celebrity/A-list adjacent reputation.

**How clients describe success:**  
"He told me exactly what I needed to hear." "The reading was so accurate." "I finally know what to do." Validation that the guidance was personal and specific, not generic.

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:**  
☑ Same day to a few days. Psychic/spiritual service decisions are emotionally driven; once a visitor trusts the practitioner, they book quickly.

**Free trial / intro offer:**  
- **Free 20-minute tarot reading page** (dedicated landing page — functions as a lead magnet or intro offer)
- **VIP SMS list** (text FUTURE to 818-600-9195) for discount codes, exclusive offers, and horoscopes

**Who/what closes the deal:**  
Self-serve online scheduling via Acuity (psychicwayne1.as.me/schedule.php) or direct phone call to 1-888-929-6307. No salesperson involved.

**#1 objection before buying:**  
Skepticism about legitimacy and accuracy — "Is this real? Will this actually help me?" Secondary: price sensitivity for longer sessions from first-time visitors.

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**The activation event:**  
The first live reading where Wayne names something specific and emotionally accurate that the client hasn't explicitly shared — the "how did he know that?" moment. This is the shift from curious visitor to true believer.

**How long / self-serve or assisted:**  
Fully assisted; happens within the first session (10–60 min depending on the booked length). Self-serve booking; Wayne delivers the experience live.

*This is the anchor for Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding — the job is to get a new subscriber to book that first reading.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?**  
Not formally — no subscription or retainer. Revenue recurs through voluntary repeat bookings. Clients return when a new life question arises or on a personally meaningful cadence (e.g., start of each year, birthday, major decision).

**Renewal / repeat cycle:**  
Irregular; client-driven. Likely clusters around life transitions and seasons (New Year, eclipses, Mercury retrograde, birthdays).

**What does expansion look like?**  
- Upgrading session length (10 min → 30/60 min)
- Adding astrology, numerology, compatibility, or transit reports alongside a base reading
- Purchasing digital tarot products (planners, e-books)

**Churn / drop-off problem:**  
One-and-done bookers who don't rebook after an initial session. VIP text and email subscribers who go cold after signing up. Crisis-motivated clients who book once and disengage once their immediate question feels resolved.

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal:**  
☑ More conversions/bookings (convert free-offer and social leads into first paid sessions) and better retention (get past clients back for repeat bookings and report upsells).

**Target metric & baseline:**  
Unknown — no public data. Target metrics to track: first-booking conversion rate from email/SMS list; repeat-booking rate; average sessions per client per year.

**Brand voice / tone:**  
Warm, mystical, and intimately personal. Authoritative credibility ("third-generation psychic to the stars," 20+ years, Hollywood A-list clients) delivered in an approachable, encouraging register — never cold or clinical. Spiritual identity is central; Wayne speaks directly to the individual's emotional state.

**Compliance constraints:**  
- Standard entertainment/spiritual services disclaimer on all email (readings for entertainment purposes)
- CAN-SPAM compliance for email
- TCPA / 10DLC compliance for SMS (active VIP text list with shortcode keyword opt-in)
- No HIPAA, financial, or legal-sector constraints

**Sending volume:**  
Unknown — determine from ESP/list audit. VIP SMS list volume unknown.
