A little about how I work

Photography that looks and feels like you.

I’m Stephanie — and I don’t shoot weddings the way most photographers do.

Instead of corralling everyone into posed groups and moving on, I move through your day quietly — camera close, eyes open — waiting for the moments that already exist. The way your partner looks at you before you see them coming down the aisle. The laugh that escapes when the vows get emotional. Your grandmother reaching for your hand without either of you noticing.

Those are the photos you’ll still be crying over in twenty years. That’s what I’m there to find.

It’s a documentary style — moody, intimate, and deeply personal. If that sounds like the kind of wedding story you want told, you’re in the right place.

“Our photos look like us — you can feel what we felt that day. Everyone who sees them tears up.”

— A past couple, exactly where you are now

No pressure, just a conversation
