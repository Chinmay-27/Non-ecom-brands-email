# Brand Intake — Stephanie April Photography
**URL:** https://stephanieapril.com  
**Date completed:** 2026-06-04  
**Vertical:** Local & service — wedding/event photography

---

## 1. The Offer

**What they sell:** Wedding photography as the core product, with engagement sessions and elopement packages as secondary offerings. An extensive add-on menu exists: bridal sessions, boudoir (solo & couples), proposal sessions, rehearsal coverage, day-after sessions, trash-the-dress, and anniversary "We Still Do" sessions.

**Core outcome the customer is buying:** Emotionally authentic, documentary-style visual memories of their wedding day — proof that the love and raw emotion they felt was real, in a form they can revisit forever.

**Price point & commitment:** One-time booking per event. $300 non-refundable retainer to secure the wedding date; full pricing not disclosed publicly (custom quotes via consultation). Based on DFW market positioning, estimated ~$1,500–$3,500+ per wedding. 65-mile travel radius from Wylie, TX (zip 75098); $0.025/mile travel fee beyond that.

**Vertical:** ☑ Local & service

---

## 2. The Audience

**Primary ICP:** Engaged couples in the Dallas-Fort Worth area — specifically Wylie, Forney, Kaufman, Terrell, Frisco, Plano — who want moody, intimate, documentary-style photography rather than traditional posed portraits. They feel deeply connected to each other and want a photographer who sees that. Also serves destination elopement couples nationwide and worldwide.

**Secondary segments:**
- Elopement couples: smaller ceremony, less traditional, often more spontaneous timeline
- Engagement session clients: often a gateway purchase that converts to a full wedding booking
- Boudoir and anniversary clients: existing-couple expansion bookings

**Problem they're in when they start looking:**
They're overwhelmed by wedding planning decisions — budget pressure, vendor overload, fear of making the wrong call — and they're looking for vendors they can trust who will make the photography side feel easy and meaningful rather than transactional.

**Lead sources:** Primarily organic search (blog SEO around DFW wedding planning topics), referrals from past couples, and likely Instagram/Pinterest visual discovery.

**How they describe success:** "Our photos look like us — you can feel what we felt that day. Everyone who sees them tears up."

---

## 3. The Sales Cycle

**Time from first contact → paying customer:** Weeks to months. Wedding photography is a considered, advance purchase — most couples book 6–18 months before their date.

**Free trial / consult / first-session offer:** Yes — **complimentary consultation** (call or meeting) to discuss the couple's vision, review products, and provide a custom quote. This is the primary sales conversion mechanism.

**Who/what closes the deal:** The consultation call → custom quote → $300 non-refundable retainer to lock the date.

**#1 objection / hesitation before buying:** Pricing opacity. There are no rates on the website, so budget-conscious couples who are comparison shopping drop off before they ever reach out. Secondary objection: uncertainty about whether Stephanie's style truly matches their vision before seeing a full gallery.

---

## 4. The "Aha" / First-Value Moment

**Activation event:** Receiving the final edited gallery — typically 6–12 weeks after the wedding — and seeing their raw, unposed emotions captured exactly as they lived them. This is the moment the investment makes complete emotional sense and drives word-of-mouth referrals.

**How long / self-serve or assisted:** Fully assisted and delayed. The value delivery is entirely in the photographer's hands post-event. Pre-event nurture matters a lot to maintain trust during the wait.

*This is also why Welcome & Onboarding matters so much — booked couples have a 6–18 month gap before the aha moment. Without nurture, that gap breeds anxiety, second-guessing, and silence.*

---

## 5. Retention & Recurring Revenue

**Is revenue recurring?** Not inherently — a wedding is a once-per-couple event. Recurring touchpoints come from life-stage milestones: engagement → wedding → anniversary → boudoir.

**Renewal / repeat cycle:** Irregular, life-event driven. An anniversary "We Still Do" session or boudoir booking can happen 1–3 years post-wedding.

**Expansion looks like:** Add-on sessions booked pre or post wedding: engagement, bridal, boudoir, rehearsal coverage, day-after, trash-the-dress, anniversary. Each is an upsell opportunity tied to the existing relationship.

**Churn / drop-off problem:**
1. Inquiry ghosting — leads who fill out a form but never hear back fast enough and book someone else
2. Budget leak — couples who need pricing reassurance and get none (no public rates)
3. Long-gap decay — booked couples who go 6–12 months without contact and start to feel like a transaction, not a priority

## 8. Goals & Constraints

**Primary goal:** More conversions/bookings (inquiry → consultation → retainer) and better retention of booked couples through the pre-wedding wait.

**Target metric:** Consultation booking rate from lead magnet downloads; inquiry-to-booking conversion rate.

**Brand voice / tone:** Romantic, poetic, emotionally intimate. Uses endearments like "Beautiful Soulmates" and "lovebirds." Empathetic to planning stress. Positions the work as personal storytelling, never a transaction. Tone mixes practical wedding guidance with heartfelt, personal sentiment — think "a best friend who also happens to be a brilliant photographer."

**Compliance constraints:** Standard CAN-SPAM email compliance. No healthcare, financial, or legal vertical constraints. Photography contracts include standard liability and retainer language.

**Sending volume:** Unknown; likely a small list appropriate for a solo photographer in a local market.

## Output: First 3 Campaigns to Build

Based on the lifecycle gaps and the default priority for a service business with a lead magnet and consultation close:

1. **Welcome & Onboarding** — post-booking nurture sequence for the 6–18 month pre-wedding gap; builds trust, reduces anxiety, reduces ghosting
2. **Lead-Magnet Nurture** — automated sequence for bridal guide downloaders converting to consultation bookings
3. **Trial/Consult Conversion** — post-inquiry follow-up for couples who reach out but haven't booked yet
