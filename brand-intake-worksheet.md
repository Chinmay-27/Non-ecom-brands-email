# Brand Intake Worksheet — Non-Ecom Email

Fill this in on the kickoff call with a new client. It tells you **who they serve, how long their cycle is, where the lifecycle gaps are, and which of the 7 backbone campaigns to build first.** One pass through this and you can place any non-ecom brand on the lifecycle map.

---

## 1. The offer
- What does the brand actually sell? (service / subscription / membership / appointments / course / donations / retainer)
- What's the core outcome the customer is buying? (the "result," not the feature)
- Price point & commitment: one-off / recurring / contract? Ticket size?
- Vertical: ☐ SaaS/tech ☐ Local & service ☐ B2B/professional ☐ Education/nonprofit/membership ☐ Other: ____

- Secondary segments worth separating:
- What problem are they in when they start looking?
- Where do leads come from today? (ads, referrals, SEO, events, walk-ins)
- How do they describe success in their own words?

## 3. The sales cycle
- Time from first contact → paying customer: ☐ same day ☐ days ☐ weeks ☐ months
- Is there a free trial, free consult, demo, or first-session offer? (Y/N — describe)
- Who/what closes the deal? (self-serve signup, a booked call, a proposal, a salesperson)
- What's the #1 objection or hesitation before buying?

## 4. The "aha" / first-value moment
- What's the single action a new customer must take to *get* the value? (the activation event)
- How long does that take? Is it self-serve or assisted?
- *(This becomes the anchor of Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding.)*

## 5. Retention & recurring revenue
- Is revenue recurring? (subscription / membership / retainer / repeat bookings)
- Renewal or repeat cycle length:
- What does "expansion" look like? (upgrade, more seats, more services, bigger gift)
- What's their churn / no-show / drop-off problem?

## 6. Data & tooling
- Email platform / ESP / CRM in use:
- Can they fire **behavioral triggers**? (signup, activation event, page visit, inactivity, renewal date)
- What signup-survey / form data do they capture? (this is your segmentation fuel — no purchase data here)
- List size & current engagement (CTR if known — ignore open rate):
- Existing lead magnet(s)?
